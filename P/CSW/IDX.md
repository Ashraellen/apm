# CSW — Church of Saint Whine

`CSW` means **Church of Saint Whine**.

Russian title:
**"Церковь" Святого Нытья**

Short description:
A compassionate satirical project built as a pseudo-sacred anti-motivation religion about whining, exhaustion, self-deception, absurd hope, and gentle release.
