# E404 — Error 404: God Not Found

`E404` means **Error 404: God Not Found**.

Russian title:
**Ошибка 404: Бог не найден**

Book I:
**Почему я? / Why Me?**

Short description:
A philosophical satire with mystical bureaucracy, domestic absurdity, pain under humor, and the movement from "Why me?" to "Who is asking?".
