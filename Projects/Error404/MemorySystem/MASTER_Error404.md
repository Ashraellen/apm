## 3. Current Book I status

Book I is completed as a 22-chapter draft.

This is no longer only a planning project.

The completed draft follows Vlad from the complaint:

`Почему я?`

toward the damaged next question:

`Кто спрашивает?`

The ending is not enlightenment, final peace or completed spiritual realization.

Correct state:

`The old claimant begins to fail.`

`The old question no longer works.`

`The old user is not found in the same form.`

Stable imported clarification:

Book I ends not with Vlad finding God, not with Vlad becoming wise, and not with a clean spiritual answer. It ends with the old applicant / old user no longer being able to operate in the previous form.

Safe final movement:

`Почему я?` → `Мне больно` → `Кто спрашивает?` → `Пользователь не найден`

## 4. Core genre and tone

Genre:

- philosophical satire;
- mystical bureaucracy;
- everyday absurdity;
- digital/apocryphal atmosphere;
- irony plus pain plus spiritual search.

Tone:

- first funny, then painful, then serious;
- living scenes, not lectures;
- humor with a philosophical knife;
- bureaucratic absurdity;
- no motivational simplification.

The book does not mock God. It mocks the human habit of turning God into service, guarantee, contract, compensation system, emergency button or supreme complaints department.

Stable late-arc rule:

`Сначала реальность, потом Канцелярия.`

Meaning:

- earthly scenes first;
- ordinary people first;
- household, work, queues, couriers, receipts, food, trash, post office, bakery, documents first;
- Chancellery flashes only after reality has already struck the nerve.

Do not let late Book I collapse back into abstract self-analysis, folder-notes, or long explanatory internal monologues.

## 5. Vlad

Vlad is the protagonist of Book I.

He is a lawyer.

He replaces the earlier name Nikolai.

At the beginning he is not a sage and not almost enlightened.

He is irritated, tired, sarcastic, offended and convinced that suffering gives him the right to demand an explanation.

He comes not for God, but for a guarantee that his version of pain will be officially recognized.

Stable imported clarification:

Vlad remains a human being, a jurist, and a damaged applicant. He must not turn into a teacher, preacher, enlightened narrator or clean carrier of correct spirituality.

His development is shown less through wisdom and more through what he no longer automatically does:

- he does not always turn experience into evidence;
- he does not always send the old request;
- he does not always open the old folder;
- he does not always grab the phone;
- he begins to suspect that the sender of the question is unclear.

## 6. Anna

Anna is Vlad's close domestic counterpart / wife figure.

Stable imported clarification:

Anna is a living earthly anchor, not a therapist, saint, spiritual teacher or authorial mouthpiece.

She may be:

- practical;
- tired;
- sharp;
- loving but not endlessly patient;
- sometimes irritated;
- sometimes unfair;
- dryly funny.

Her function is to keep Vlad in living reality: bread, coffee, stove, shelf, trash, dishes, laundry, parcels, body, fatigue, shared life.

She should cut through Vlad's legal-metaphysical constructions with ordinary life, not explain the metaphysics for him.

## 7. Poshpshekhon

Poshpshekhon appears in Book I in the Donner Kebab scene.

Stable imported clarification:

He is a one-time ordinary bearer of a living seed, not a guru, coach, lecturer, spiritual performer, recurring mentor or mystical guide.

He should leave a splinter in Vlad's perception, not deliver a lesson.

The Donner Kebab scene must remain earthly, ordinary, funny and exact.

Core idea connected with him:

`God does not hide. A human being comes with a box and demands that infinity fit inside it.`

This idea leads into the later box motif but must not turn Poshpshekhon into a permanent teacher.

## 8. Heavenly Chancellery

The Heavenly Chancellery is not a place, institution, office, app or fantasy bureaucracy.

It is a state through which a person encounters their own request to God, life, fate or Truth.

For Vlad, it appears through a legal-bureaucratic interface: claims, windows, numbers, notifications, categories of appeals.

Stable imported clarification:

The Chancellery is a state / interface, not an application and not a literal office.

It does not leave evidence fit for complaint.

It does not comfort or perform miracles as spectacle.

It registers, sorts, redirects, clarifies and exposes the hidden motive behind the beautiful wording.

Safe formulas:

`Канцелярия не оставляет доказательств, пригодных для жалобы.`

`Канцелярия не наказывает. Она фиксирует.`

## 9. Window No. 7

Window No. 7 is not a physical office.

It opens in a state.

For Vlad, it handles the category:

`Почему я?` / `Why me?`

The waiting hall is a place where lonelinesses sit next to each other.

The conversations are non-conversations: inner monologues intersect Vlad's questions without becoming normal dialogue.

## 10. Book II — stable current direction

Working title:

`На всё воля Его`

Possible English working title:

`On All Things His Will`

Main protagonist:

A priest / minister / religious servant of undefined confession.

Stable safe direction:

Book II is not Vlad-centred.

The camera shifts to another person from the larger Chancellery queue / cycle.

The Book II protagonist has lived inside religious form and knows it from within. He should be sincere, intelligent, disciplined, pedantic and frightened — not a caricature, not a stupid fanatic and not an anti-religious target.

Core fracture:

He has used the formula:

`На всё воля Его.`

At first this formula works as comfort, discipline, pastoral restraint and a way to keep people from collapse.

Then the word `всё` becomes absolute.

Main question:

`If absolutely everything is His will, where does sin come from?`

Chancellery interface for Book II:

Not screen.

Not phone notification.

Not Vlad's digital/legal interface.

For the priest, the Chancellery manifests as a second layer of thought:

`A thought appears in him, but not from him.`

This direction is safe to keep because it aligns with current root cycle canon.
