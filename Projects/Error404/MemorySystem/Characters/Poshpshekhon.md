# Poshpshekhon — Character Memory

Status: initial memory
Created: 2026-06-22

## Core

Poshpshekhon appears as an ordinary carrier of a living grain.

He should not be portrayed as a guru, coach, mystical performer or lecturer.

He and his companion have already passed through a path similar to Vlad's, but they simply live ordinary lives.

## Function

He may say something simple that enters Vlad's life as an answer, but he does not explain reality as a teacher.

## Donner Kebab rule

The Donner Kebab scene should remain ordinary, funny, earthly and exact.

No spiritual performance.

No guru aura.

## Important phrase / idea

A person brings a box and demands that infinity fit into it.

This leads toward the later `Коробка` chapter and Vlad's literal sorting of old boxes with Anna.
