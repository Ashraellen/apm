# Poshpshekhon — Character Memory

Status: active local character memory
Created: 2026-06-22
Updated: 2026-06-22

## Core

Poshpshekhon appears as an ordinary carrier of a living grain.

He should not be portrayed as a guru, coach, mystical performer, lecturer, recurring mentor or spiritual guide.

He and his companion have already passed through a path similar to Vlad's, but they simply live ordinary lives.

## Function

He may say something simple that enters Vlad's life as an answer, but he does not explain reality as a teacher.

His function is not to enlighten Vlad.

His function is to damage Vlad's old interface of perception.

He leaves a splinter, not a lecture.

## Donner Kebab rule

The Donner Kebab scene should remain ordinary, funny, earthly and exact.

No spiritual performance.

No guru aura.

No mystical clairvoyance show.

No recurring mentorship unless the author explicitly changes this later.

## Important phrase / idea

A person brings a box and demands that infinity fit into it.

This leads toward the later `Коробка` chapter and Vlad's literal sorting of old boxes with Anna.

Safe formulation of the core idea:

`Бог не прячется. Это человек приходит с коробкой и требует, чтобы бесконечность в неё поместилась.`

## Boundary

Do not make Poshpshekhon a convenient explanation-delivery device.

Do not make him solve Vlad.

After Donner Kebab, the phrase continues to work inside Vlad's life, but Poshpshekhon should not become a permanent guide.
