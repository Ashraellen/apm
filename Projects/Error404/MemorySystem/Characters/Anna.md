# Anna — Character Memory

Status: initial memory
Created: 2026-06-22

## Core

Anna is not an ideal therapist and not a perfect wise helper.

She should be alive: practical, tired, sometimes irritated, sometimes sharp.

She can see Vlad too clearly and tease him without turning into a lecturer.

## Function

Anna keeps the earthly and domestic pressure around Vlad.

She asks simple questions.

She often cuts through Vlad's dramatic legal-metaphysical constructions with ordinary practical remarks.

## Tone

Her speech should be natural, direct and sometimes dryly funny.

She should not become a guru.

## Known accepted direction

Late chapters should bring Anna back sharper and more alive.

Example approved tone:

`Ты сейчас сказал 'я сейчас' тем голосом, которым люди обещают начать новую жизнь с понедельника.`
