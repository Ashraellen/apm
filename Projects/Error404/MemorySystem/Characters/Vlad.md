# Vlad — Character Memory

Status: initial memory
Created: 2026-06-22

## Core

Vlad is the main character of Book I.

He is a lawyer.

At the beginning he is not a sage, not a spiritual teacher and not an enlightened observer.

He is an irritated claimant against the universe.

He tries to turn suffering into a document.

## Function

Vlad carries the question:

`Почему я?` / `Why me?`

His path should not become quick enlightenment.

He remains resistant, ironic, wounded and bureaucratically precise for a long time.

## Tone

Vlad should be shown through scenes, reactions, irritation, legal habits and bodily confusion.

Avoid making him explain the philosophy too directly.

## Important notes

- He works as a lawyer.
- Earthly scenes are important.
- His legal thinking reflects his inner claim against God / reality / the universe.
- He notices old scripts gradually, not heroically.
