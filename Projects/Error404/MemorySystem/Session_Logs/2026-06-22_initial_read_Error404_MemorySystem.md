## 2. Current understanding

`Error 404: God Not Found` / `Ошибка 404: Бог не найден` is a multilingual philosophical-satirical novel cycle / series, not a fixed trilogy. Each future book may function as a separate Heavenly Chancellery case.

Book I, `Почему я? / Why Me?`, is completed as a 22-chapter draft. It follows Vlad, a lawyerly claimant against Creation, from the question `Почему я?` toward the damaged threshold question `Кто спрашивает?`. The ending is not enlightenment or healing; the old claimant identity begins to fail and the old user is no longer found in the same form.

Book II working title: `На всё воля Его` / possible English title `On All Things His Will`. It is not Vlad-centred; the central protagonist is a priest / minister of undefined confession whose formula `На всё воля Его` fractures when the word `всё` becomes absolute.

The Heavenly Chancellery is not a literal place, office, app, or comic bureaucracy. It is a state through which each protagonist encounters their own distorted request to God, life, fate, or Truth. For Vlad it appears through legal-bureaucratic surfaces; for the priest it appears as a second layer of thought.
