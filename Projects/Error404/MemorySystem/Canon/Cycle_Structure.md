## Core structure

`Error 404: God Not Found` / `Ошибка 404: Бог не найден` is currently a multilingual philosophical-satirical novel cycle / series.

It should not be treated only as a strict Vlad-centered trilogy.

Earlier trilogy logic remains useful as internal philosophical movement and future reservoir, but the current external form is a cycle / series of Chancellery cases.

## General formula

Each book may function as a separate case of the Heavenly Chancellery.

Each protagonist searches for God, Truth, justice, meaning or an answer through a distorted form convenient to their own fear, pain, faith, profession, knowledge, control or self-deception.

Safe formula:

`God is not “not found” because He is absent. God is not found because the person searches for Him in the wrong mode.`

## Book I

Title:

`Почему я?` / `Why Me?`

Main protagonist:

`Vlad`

Status:

Completed 22-chapter draft.

Book I opens the cycle through Vlad, a lawyerly claimant against Creation.

Main movement:

`Почему я?` → `Мне больно` → `Кто спрашивает?` → `Пользователь не найден`

Final state:

- not enlightenment;
- not full healing;
- not spiritual teaching;
- not Vlad becoming a guru;
- old applicant / old user fails in the previous form.

## Book II

Working title:

`На всё воля Его`

Possible English working title:

`On All Things His Will`

Main protagonist:

A priest / minister / religious servant of undefined confession.

Safe current direction:

Book II is not Vlad-centred.

Its central fracture is the formula:

`На всё воля Его.`

The word `всё` becomes absolute and opens the question:

`If absolutely everything is His will, where does sin come from?`

Chancellery interface:

For the priest, the Chancellery does not appear mainly through screens, phone notifications, apps or Vlad's legal-digital interface.

It appears as a second layer of thought:

`A thought appears in him, but not from him.`

## Future books

Future books may follow new protagonists and new forms of not finding God.

Possible future seed-types already safe to remember:

- woman with thermos — waiting / help / burnout;
- scientist with three pens — knowledge / control / accounting for everything;
- possible atheist / denier who still waits for an answer;
- possible mother who confuses love and control;
- person who received a miracle but rejected it because of unacceptable packaging.

These are not fixed future book contracts. They are seeds / reservoir.

## Boundary

Do not force Vlad to remain the protagonist of every future book.

Do not convert the cycle into an abstract theological treatise.

Do not make future protagonists teaser cards.

Future protagonists must appear as living people, not advertised franchise slots.
