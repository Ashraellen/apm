## 2. Accepted decisions

### 2.1 Book I structure

- Book I title: `Почему я? / Why Me?`.
- Book I has **22 chapters**.
- The final movement of Book I is not toward full enlightenment, but toward the collapse of the old question.
- Final vector of Book I:
  - from `Почему я?`
  - through `Мне больно`
  - through `Заявитель / отправитель не подтверждён`
  - to `Кто спрашивает?`
- The first book should not answer everything. It should break the old interface.

### 2.2 Late arc after Donner Kebab

Accepted late-arc rule after the meeting with Poshpshekhon:

- Vlad is angry because he senses that Poshpshekhon and the man with the dark braid are not simple, but they do not give him a convenient way either to accept them as teachers or reject them as charlatans.
- After the phrase about the box, Vlad begins to see that his search for God was an attempt to pack infinity into his own box.
- Late chapters must not return to kitchen/office self-analysis loops.
- Late chapters must be dynamic, funny, externally eventful, with Anna, clients, couriers, queues, post offices, service points, ordinary people, printers, parcels, trash, food, and everyday absurdity.
- Core late-arc rule: **Сначала реальность, потом Канцелярия.**
- Reduced use of the folder `Личное`; it may appear as temptation or old mechanism, but must not again become the main action site.

### 2.3 Style decisions

- Genre formula accepted: **балаган + философский нож**.
- Not a spiritual tract.
- The book should first make the reader laugh, then suddenly show their own complaint against God.
- Keep the mystical through everyday objects and procedures: phone, mirror, post office, return form, receipt, bread, chocolate, queue, kitchen, trash, old building, official window.
- Avoid lectures and overly direct explanations.
- Avoid text where characters seem aware they are in a novel/chapter/story.
- Remove meta-fictional intrusions like `вопрос всего романа`, `редактировал главу`, `после двадцати одной главы`.
- Characters must not know they are in a novel.
- Replace meta phrases with internally plausible phrases.

### 2.4 Accepted formulae / core phrases

- `Запрос не отправлен. Причина: отправитель не подтверждён.`
- `Отправитель находится в уточнении.`
- `Вопрос воспроизведён. Живое содержание не обнаружено.`
- `Состояние принято. Это не вопрос.`
- `Правда не всегда является обращением.`
- `Присутствуете при состоянии.`
- `Ошибка 404 указывала не на отсутствие Бога. Продолжение недоступно в данном окне.`
- `Прежний пользователь не найден.`
- `Для продолжения уточните: кто спрашивает?`
- `Влад даже не задумался, почему они пришли так. Возможно, здесь это было неважно.`

## 4. Characters

### Vlad

- Jurist, not mystic in beginning.
- Starts as plaintiff/user/complainant against the Universe.
- Main old question: `Почему я?`
- Gradually sees that `Почему я?` was a packaging for `Мне больно`, demand for recognition, and desire for a certified copy of pain.
- By end of Book I, old user is not found; Vlad remains alive, less attached to old complaint.
- He does not become enlightened. He becomes less able to use God as respondent.
- Key late movement: no longer grabs phone immediately; can leave folder `Личное` unopened; can not send the old request.

### Anna

- Wife of Vlad.
- Not ideal therapist, not saint, not guru.
- Alive, tired, sharp, sometimes irritated, sometimes tender.
- Earth anchor.
- Returns Vlad to life: bread, chocolate, trash, kitchen, body, presence.
- Should be stronger and more alive in late chapters.
- Example tone:
  - `Главное, чтобы мусор был отправлен.`
  - `Ты сейчас не отвечаешь. Ты защищаешься перед человеком, который спросил, когда мы будем дома.`
  - `Если бы я работала в Канцелярии, у вас бы давно навели порядок.`
- Anna should not become explanatory spiritual function.

### Poshpshekhon and the man with dark braid

- Already enlightened in quiet, ordinary, non-commercial way.
- Not gurus, not coaches, not mystical performers.
- Poshpshekhon appears once, ordinary and sharp, connected to Donner Kebab.
- His phrase about the box triggers the late arc.
- Vlad is angry because he cannot safely classify them.
- Poshpshekhon may not need to appear again physically; his phrase continues to work.

### David

- Shows the letter written by a wound.
- Wants external recognition to restore self.
- Chapter 20 key file: `Ответ_финал_последний_точно.docx`.
- Vlad teaches not to send from the place that wants to win the last internal trial.

### Maria

- Shows the desire for documents to confirm pain was not in vain.
- Brings apples in chapter 19.
- Status `человек, который принёс яблоки` is more human than legal role.
- Learns not to answer from the old wound.

### Nick

- Represents fatigue of being adult/reasonable.
- Helps show that reason is boring but may avoid increasing damage.

### Alexander

- Represents desire for recognition and confirming one’s own version.

### Random people

- Must remain alive, ordinary, not merely symbolic.
- Their ordinary phrases often hit Vlad’s central nerve.
- They should not be turned into “teaching aids” or NPCs for Vlad.

## 5. Canon / universe

### Core universe

- `Небесная Канцелярия` is not exactly a place and not an app.
- It is an interface of state, showing through phone, TV, windows, mirror, signs, queues, documents, notifications, ordinary coincidences.
- It appears when a person tries to formalize pain as claim.
- It should be dry, bureaucratic, polite, merciless, never syrupy.

### God

- God is not a character giving final explanation.
- God is not found as an object because God is not an object.
- Book I must not end with `Влад понял, что Бог всегда был внутри` — this is too sticker-like and false in tone.
- Correct final shift: God not found as object; old user not found; error has changed.

### Folder `Личное`

- Old mechanism of documentation and control.
- In late arc it must weaken.
- It may stand on shelf and tempt, but Vlad increasingly does not open it.
- Do not let folder again become main temple of inner accounting.

### Phone

- Interface of Канцелярия.
- In chapters 21–22, Vlad’s ability to not check phone becomes major shift.
- Phone may glow, but he can leave it.

### Mirror

- `Отдел идентификации заявителя`.
- Mirror accepts name/body/role/profession/pain/etc., but not applicant.
- It should not become simple magic portal; it is a verification interface.

### Receipt / check

- Symbol of confirmation and proof.
- In final chapter Vlad buys without receipt; world does not collapse.

### Glove

- Final quiet image: good without recipient, account, gratitude, proof.
- Child glove lifted from dirt and placed on bench.

### Window No. 7 — key mechanism

- `Окно №7` is an interface of the Chancellery for appeals in the category `Почему я?`.
- It is not an ordinary place.
- For Vlad it appears as a reception room with institutional chairs because he translates pain into bureaucratic language.
- People at Window No. 7 are present by their state/appeal/consciousness; this must not be explained directly in prose.
- They sit on ordinary chairs but carry traces of their own real places through clothes, objects, and phrases.
- No direct dialogue; people self-talk.
- Their self-talk sometimes intersects and functions as crooked answer to another’s question.
- Do not make everyone answer Vlad.
- `Следующий` is called and someone rises as if it is understood that they must enter.
- Keep ordinary reception; no surreal furniture warehouse.
