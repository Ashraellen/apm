## 5. Canon / universe

### 5.1. Window №7 / Окно №7

This chat clarified Window №7 across multiple phases.

Core accepted principle:

- Window №7 is not simply a place.
- It is a state/form of appeal under the category “Почему я?” / “Why me?”
- It manifests differently depending on Vlad’s state.

Three modes identified:

#### Mode 1 — First application / primary complaint

Chapter 2.

- Form: large hall, queue, ticket number, category “Почему я?”
- Vlad’s state: claimant / plaintiff.
- Question: “Почему я?”
- Function: to show that Vlad’s pain is real but his application is standard.
- Risk: visitors must not become ordinary conversational partners.

#### Mode 2 — Identification surface

Chapter 19.

- Form: mirror / matte surface / “Отдел идентификации заявителя.”
- Vlad’s state: the claimant begins to fail as a stable identity.
- Question shifts from “Почему я?” to “Кто это — я?”
- Function: separate body, name, role, profession, pain, correctness, and suffering from the applicant.

#### Mode 3 — Repeated application

Chapter 21.

- Form: ordinary administrative building, entrance from courtyard, small waiting room, grey lamp, repeated Window №7.
- Vlad’s state: old question no longer works in the same way.
- Key procedure: repeated application possible only if question or applicant changes.
- Function: distinguish pain, truth, question, demand, and presence.

### 5.2. Cotton hall / non-conversation rule

Accepted and applied in chapter 2 and chapter 21:

- People in Window №7 do not hold normal conversations.
- Vlad’s questions do not create direct dialogue.
- The hall is acoustically and existentially “cottoned”: voices sink, do not bounce back, and do not form ordinary social exchange.
- Visitors speak from their own inner state.
- Their monologues may intersect Vlad’s questions and function as answers, but not as direct replies.

### 5.3. Heavenly Chancellery / Небесная Канцелярия

Reaffirmed rules:

- The Chancellery is not simply an app, place, or physical office.
- It manifests through surfaces appropriate to Vlad’s perception: phone, mirror, door, queue, receipt, administrative building, etc.
- It does not need to leave usable evidence.
- It should not become cheap poltergeist or theatrical mysticism.
- Its language should remain dry, polite, precise, bureaucratic, and spiritually unpleasant.

### 5.4. Form without physical trace

Chapter 21 added a refined manifestation rule:

- In Window №7, actions and objects may appear without ordinary physical consequences.
- Example: a man smokes, smoke is visible, but there is no smell of tobacco.
- This should be used subtly.

### 5.5. “Truth is not always an appeal”

Chapter 21 canon/logic:

- “Господи. Мне больно.” is true, but not necessarily an administrative request.
- Pain does not require completed identification to exist.
- But a question/request requires an identifiable sender.
- “Мне больно” can be presence at a state, not a demand.

Important formulas:

```text
Состояние принято.
Это не вопрос.
```

```text
Правда не всегда является обращением.
```

```text
Присутствуете при состоянии.
```

```text
Пока не отправляю.
```
