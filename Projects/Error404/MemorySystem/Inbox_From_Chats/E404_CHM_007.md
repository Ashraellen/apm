## 2. Accepted decisions

### 2.1 Project structure: cycle instead of strict trilogy

Accepted decision:

**Error 404: God Not Found** should no longer be treated only as a strict trilogy centered on Vlad.

It is now better understood as a **cycle / serial of novels**, where each book may follow a different protagonist and a different form of human failure in searching for God, Truth, justice, meaning, or answer.

Working formula:

```text
Each book is a Chancellery case about a person who searches for God in a form convenient to their fear.
```

Another accepted formula:

```text
God is not found not because He is absent,
but because the person searches for Him as an object, address, institution, confirmation, proof, guarantee, or service department.
```

Vlad remains important, but he is not required to be the protagonist of all future books.

### 2.2 Fixed current books

Current fixed books:

```text
Book I:  Почему я? / Why Me?
Book II: На всё воля Его
```

Future books:

```text
As the Chancellery gives.
```

### 2.3 Book II protagonist

Accepted:

Book II protagonist is a **priest / clergyman / religious servant of undefined confession**.

He should be:

- sincere;
- intelligent;
- disciplined;
- pedantic;
- frightened;
- not a caricature;
- not a stupid fanatic;
- not an anti-religious target;
- not a religious clown.

He is a person who has lived inside religious form and knows it from within.

The confession should stay deliberately undefined enough for multilingual / cross-cultural usability, though the Russian source layer may carry Orthodox resonance because of the author's personal experience as a deacon.

### 2.4 Book II title

Accepted Russian title:

```text
На всё воля Его
```

English working senses:

```text
All Is His Will
It Is All His Will
```

Final English title is not locked.

### 2.5 Book II core conflict

Accepted core:

The priest has long used the formula:

```text
На всё воля Его.
```

At first it functions as consolation, discipline, pastoral restraint, and a way to keep people from falling apart.

One day the word **всё** becomes absolute.

Main question:

```text
If absolutely everything is His will, where does sin come from?
```

The priest enters Window №7 not through Vlad's legal complaint, but through a theological fracture.

### 2.6 Sin / restraint-system idea

Accepted conceptual basis:

If absolutely everything is God's will, then sin in the simplified church-pedagogical sense cannot exist as an autonomous force outside God's will.

The church concept of sin may function as a system of restraint / checks and balances that helps people hold themselves together.

Formula from conversation:

```text
People hold themselves by the little pants.
```

This should be treated as an underlying mechanism, not necessarily used literally in the novel.

The book should show that a system can be useful, protective, and socially/spiritually restraining while still not being the Absolute Truth.

### 2.7 No anti-religious pamphlet, but edge of rupture

Accepted:

Book II must **not** become an anti-religious pamphlet.

But it must not become a safe respectful theological essay either.

Rule:

```text
Do not attack God.
Do not flatten faith.
Do not mock living belief.
But do not soften the crack to protect the form.
```

The book must go along the edge of rupture. The danger comes from the fact that the question is born inside faith.

### 2.8 Priest's breakdown / besnovanie

Accepted:

The priest will sincerely classify the Chancellery-thoughts as temptation / demonic thought / spiritual delusion / pride / dangerous reasoning.

He begins to rage / inwardly “besnovatsya” not because he is possessed by a demon, but because he cannot understand how a thought that feels more alive than his defense can be dangerous, and how a question that threatens doctrine can also feel closer to God.

Key formulas:

```text
He does not become possessed by a demon.
He becomes possessed by the defense against the demon.
```

```text
He fights not the devil, but the question he cannot exorcise.
```

```text
He prays not for God to answer,
but for the question to stop.
```

### 2.9 Chancellery manifestation for the priest

Accepted:

For Book II there should be **no screen** as main interface.

The screen belongs more naturally to Vlad's legal-digital world.

For the priest, the Chancellery manifests as a **second layer of thought**:

- not a voice;
- not a vision;
- not text on screen;
- not external speech;
- not a hallucinated interface.

Working formula:

```text
A thought appears in him, but not from him.
It does not sound.
It is simply already there.
```

The thoughts are calm, complete, precise, inward, and impossible to dismiss honestly.

### 2.10 The small book and the notebook

Accepted:

In Book I Chapter 2, the future priest should not visibly hold a standalone squared notebook.

Instead:

- he holds a small worn book;
- the book resembles a prayer book / small religious book / pocket text used not merely for reading, but for holding oneself inside boundaries;
- a thin notebook is inserted between the pages;
- Vlad cannot see that the notebook is squared / in grid;
- the grid notebook detail is reserved for Book II when the camera shifts to the priest.

Symbolic reading:

```text
The small book = allowed / official form of faith.
The inserted notebook = hidden investigation.
The squared notebook in Book II = attempt to place infinity into a grid.
```

Accepted formula:

```text
He tries to put infinity into a grid.
```

Accepted canonical address forms:

Reaffirmed:

The Heavenly Chancellery is not a normal location, office, app, or simple supernatural department.

It manifests through the perceptual / conceptual interface of the protagonist.

### 5.2 Interface changes by protagonist

Vlad:

```text
legal-bureaucratic / digital / phone / window / category / applicant / notification
```

Priest:

```text
second layer of thought / paternal inward address / small book / notebook / discernment / temptation classification
```

Scientist candidate:

```text
likely through formulas, hypotheses, models, measurement, contradiction between calculation and life — not fixed yet
```

Woman with thermos candidate:

```text
likely through waiting, warmth, help, care, burnout, sessions, practices, readiness for long waiting — not fixed yet
```

### 5.3 Window №7 rule

Window №7 is not just a queue.

It is a state where people sit together but remain inside their own questions.

The phrases spoken there are not ordinary dialogue. They are fragments of inner monologues. Sometimes someone else's inner monologue hits another person's question.

### 5.4 Future-cycle principle

Accepted:

Each future book should reveal a new form of not finding God:

- legal complaint;
- theological formula;
- helping others instead of meeting oneself;
- scientific totalization / control;
- possible atheistic resentment;
- possible love/control confusion;
- possible unacceptable miracle.

### 5.5 Do not overpopulate Chapter 2

Accepted warning:

Chapter 2 can seed future characters, but should not become a trailer/casting scene.

Future heroes should appear as ordinary people in the queue, not as advertised protagonists.
