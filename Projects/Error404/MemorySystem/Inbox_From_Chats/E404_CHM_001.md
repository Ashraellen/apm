## 1. Summary

This chat belongs to the book project **Error 404: God Not Found** / **Ошибка 404: Бог не найден**.

Core achievement of the chat: the project was defined as a humorous-philosophical novel about a man who tries to file a complaint against God, gradually discovering that his question is not really about God but about his own demand for recognition, proof, and confirmation of suffering.

In this chat:

- the original book concept was shaped;
- the first four chapters were written and exported as files;
- the title structure was changed: **Error 404: God Not Found** became the series/cycle title, while Book I became **Почему я? / Why Me?**;
- Vlad was defined as a lawyer and a claimant against the Universe;
- the Heavenly Chancellery and Window No. 7 were defined;
- the rule of non-dialogue / self-talk around Window No. 7 was refined;
- the principle of disappearing Chancellery notifications was defined;
- the principle of answer-through-synchronization with external ordinary speech was defined;
- Poshpshekhon and the Donner Kebab scene were planned and later evaluated after chapter 12 was uploaded from another chat;
- chapter 13 **Коробка** was planned as the next post-Donner Kebab earthly chapter;
- a LORE & Universe draft for three books was created in this chat as a conceptual document.

This raw memory must be sorted against the current root canon before being moved into any master file.

## 2. Accepted decisions

### 2.1 Series and Book I titles

Accepted title structure:

- **Series / cycle:** `Error 404: God Not Found`
- Russian working variant of series: `Ошибка 404: Бог не найден`
- **Book I:** `Почему я?`
- English Book I title: `Why Me?`

Rationale accepted in chat:

- `Error 404: God Not Found` is strong, conceptual, ironic, and works better as the series/cycle title.
- `Почему я? / Why Me?` is more direct, human, painful, and better as the title of the first book because it names Vlad’s primary complaint and the Window No. 7 category.

### 2.2 File naming rule

Accepted file naming format:

```text
E404_GNF_Book1_Why_Me_Glava_01_Oshibka_404.md
E404_GNF_Book1_Why_Me_Glava_01_Oshibka_404.docx
E404_GNF_Book1_Why_Me_Glava_01_Oshibka_404.txt
```

Rules:

- chapter number always two digits: `01`, `02`, `03`, etc.;
- chapter title in Latin transliteration / English without spaces;
- spaces replaced by underscores;
- add `v2`, `v3` etc. for revised versions;
- each finished chapter should exist in three formats: `.md`, `.docx`, `.txt`.

Examples fixed in the canon update:


### 2.3 Territorial neutrality

Accepted rule: the novel should be multilingual-friendly and must avoid concrete national or territorial anchoring.

Avoid:

- specific countries;
- specific cities;
- local politics;
- local legal systems;
- local bureaucracy;
- recognizable national legal procedure unless needed later and deliberately.

Use instead:

- a nameless city;
- an office;
- kitchen;
- apartment;
- street;
- pharmacy;
- temple;
- kebab place;
- old building;
- wet asphalt;
- clients;
- documents;
- household scenes;
- universal legal concepts.

### 2.4 Names

Accepted:

- Main character: **Влад**.
- Chapter 4 client was corrected from **Артём** to **Александр** because Alexander is more internationally transferable and neutral.
- In English, Alexander can remain `Alexander`.

Rule: secondary names should be neutral and easy to translate / adapt.

## 3.3 Chapter 3 — Демон корректных формулировок

Written in this chat and revised repeatedly.

Core accepted idea:

- Vlad, as a lawyer, tries to rewrite his complaint so perfectly that the Heavenly Chancellery cannot reject it.
- He writes on paper.
- He tries to turn pain into a legally impeccable document.
- He does not use a magical app.
- Phone may show rare disappearing notifications, but the main battle is in his language and intention.
- Paper remains paper; no mystical lines appear on it.
- Kettle is removed as an active answer-object for this chapter.

Important rejected/outdated elements:

- text appearing on kettle;
- text appearing on paper;
- Vlad waiting for objects to answer;
- too much kitchen-object metaphysics;
- direct TV speaking to Vlad personally.

Accepted mechanism:

- TV/radio/news provide ordinary phrases that synchronize with Vlad’s inner question.
- Physically everyone hears the same words, but Vlad hears an answer because the phrase coincides with his inner question.
- Vlad does not simply switch off the TV; as a lawyer, he tries to test, record, verify, and reproduce the synchronization.
- The recording proves the phrase existed but cannot prove it was an answer.

Key internal turn:

- Vlad reaches the word `признать`.
- He realizes the hidden request is:

```text
Признать, что заявитель страдал не зря.
```

End movement:

- Vlad writes:

```text
Что я пытаюсь доказать?
```

This is the first question aimed at himself rather than at God/the system.

## 4. Characters

## 4.1 Vlad

- Main protagonist.
- Lawyer.
- Not a sage at start; he is claimant, irritated client of the Universe, professional of formulations.
- Attempts to file pain as a document.
- Starts from the complaint: `Почему я?`
- His central weakness: wants recognition that his suffering was not meaningless and that his version is valid.
- His professional reflex: proof, document, formulation, counterparty, recognition, status, admissible evidence.
- Repeated path markers:
  - `Что я пытаюсь доказать?`
  - `Кому?`
  - `Кому я всё время предъявляю?`
  - `Бог не инспектор. А я?`
  - `Просил Бога / Просил у Бога`
  - `Спасибо`
  - `Помог — не тащи потом чек`

Vlad must not become wise too early. He resists, jokes, defends, formulates, appropriates, and tries to turn living truth into material.

## 4.2 Anna

- Vlad’s close person / partner at home.
- Must not be ideal therapist.
- Alive, sometimes tired, practical, sharp, human.
- Returns Vlad to household reality.
- Key line: `Я не на суде, Влад.`
- Her function is not to explain God but to expose Vlad’s process-language in living relationships.
- Uses humor and practical clarity: lamp, cupboard, coffee, parcel, lists, household tasks.

## 4.3 Alexander

- Chapter 4 client.
- Neutral name chosen for multilingual portability.
- Reflects Vlad’s issue: wants not only money/debt settlement but recognition, apology, moral restoration.
- Used to reveal that recognition cannot be legally extracted.

## 4.4 Poshpshekhon / Владислав Павленко

- Real friend/teacher’s spiritual name used in novel as one-time ordinary enlightened figure.
- In fiction: not guru, not teacher, not course leader, not scheduled mentor.
- Appears once, casually, in Donner Kebab.
- Light-haired, short haircut, ordinary appearance.
- Speaks sharply, directly, with rough precision.
- Does not “know” Vlad mystically.
- Should not become recurring mentor.
- Function: deliver living answer in unacceptable packaging.

Key Poshpshekhon ideas:

- Vlad is stuck in a layer of scripts.
- “Why me?”, “what for?”, “let him understand,” etc. are often rhetorical scripts, not real questions.
- Mind gets covered by incoming files / burrs / spam traffic.
- Human suffering is often generated by assigning significance and then attaching catastrophe scenarios.
- Person can suffer over nearly anything once meaning is assigned.
- The system treats such questions as spam if they do not become genuine questions.

## 4.5 Second man with braid

- Authorial silhouette in Donner Kebab.
- Larger, dark long braid, calm.
- Name Ashraellen should not be explicitly inserted.
- He names Poshpshekhon in overheard speech.
- After that mostly eats / observes / says very few exact lines.
- Not a mystical demonstrator; presence, not performance.

## 5. Canon / universe

## 5.1 Heavenly Chancellery

- Not a place in normal sense.
- It opens as a state/interface.
- For Vlad it appears bureaucratic/legal because he is lawyer/claimant.
- It is not an app.
- It uses any surface of reality but must not become a fantasy magic system.
- It does not leave evidence admissible for complaint.
- Its purpose is not comfort but precision.

## 5.2 Disappearing notifications

Accepted canon:

- Not from an app.
- No sender.
- No icon.
- No history.
- No screenshot-proof / or screenshots fail to prove meaning.
- Exist only in moment of perception.
- If read, remain only in Vlad.
- If missed, disappear.
- Key formula:

```text
Канцелярия не оставляет доказательств, пригодных для жалобы.
```

## 5.3 Answer through synchronization

Accepted canon:

- External world does not physically change words for each person.
- Everyone hears the same phrase.
- The answer occurs when an ordinary external phrase coincides with an inner question.
- Example: TV news phrase about recognition/liability/demands hits Vlad because it matches his written appeal.
- Others hear only ordinary news.
- Distinction is not sound but context of consciousness.

## 5.4 Window No. 7 — current rule

Important latest correction:

Do **not** use older descriptions:

- gray cotton wool;
- voice sinking;
- sound not reaching;
- acoustic capsule;
- voice falling into silence.

Current accepted version:

- Vlad speaks aloud.
- His voice exists.
- Others do not hear it as an address to themselves.
- There is no normal dialogue.
- People speak as if to themselves.
- Their self-talk can fully/partially/crookedly answer Vlad’s question.
- They do not become interlocutors.
- The scene is not a queue chat but a set of parallel self-conversations.

Formula:

```text
Влад задаёт вопрос вслух.
Другой человек не слышит его как вопрос к себе.
Но из самобеседы этого человека может прозвучать фраза, которая частично или полностью отвечает Владу.
```

## 5.5 Layer of scripts

Accepted concept from Poshpshekhon discussion:

- Window No. 7 is not deep truth but a layer of primary rhetorical scripts.
- “Why me?” is initially not a real question but a pain-script.
- Scripts resemble questions but do not seek answers; they seek feeding/repetition.
- The mind can be filled with incoming script-files / burrs / internal spam.

Working formula:

```text
Некоторые мысли не думаются. Они запускаются.
```

Use in prose via scenes, not lectures.

## 5.6 Box

Accepted post-Donner Kebab symbol:

- Box must remain everyday, not decorative allegory.
- After Poshpshekhon’s phrase, Vlad should physically encounter boxes.
- Box `Разное` is the bodily form of the script layer.
- It contains not just things but evidence, old versions, reasons not to let go.

Working formula:

```text
В коробку не помещается Бог. Зато туда прекрасно помещается всё, чем человек доказывает, что имел право не меняться.
```

## 5.7 Do not overuse metaphysics

Accepted style rule:

- Do not make objects speak too much.
- No kettle writing lines.
- No paper self-writing in early Book I.
- No TV directly addressing Vlad personally.
- Prefer ordinary life as exact answer.
- After big mystical chapters, use earthly consequence chapters.
