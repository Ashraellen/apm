# Polish Rules — Error404

Status: initial rules
Created: 2026-06-22

## Principle

Polish translation should be careful literary transcreation, not mechanical translation.

It should sound natural in Polish while preserving meaning, tone, structure and pressure of the Russian master version.

## Rules

- do not simplify;
- do not shorten;
- do not smooth away irony or pain;
- preserve mystical bureaucracy and everyday absurdity;
- preserve Vlad's legal irritation;
- avoid turning the text into motivational prose.
