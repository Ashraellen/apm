## 1. Краткая общая сводка

The imported chats cover the full working arc of Book I and the transition from the older trilogy model to the current cycle / series model.

Main imported memory:

1. The project began as a humorous-philosophical novel about a man filing a complaint against God.
2. Book I was shaped around Vlad, a lawyerly claimant who tries to formalize suffering as a document.
3. Book I title is `Почему я? / Why Me?`; the cycle title is `Error 404: God Not Found / Ошибка 404: Бог не найден`.
4. Book I is fixed as a 22-chapter draft.
5. Early chapters established the complaint, Window №7, the Demon of Correct Formulations, Vlad’s legal reality, prayer/accounting/miracle motifs, and Donner Kebab.
6. The late arc was rebuilt after Donner Kebab because the first version was too introspective, too repetitive, and too dependent on `Личное` / inner notes.
7. The accepted late-arc principle became: **Сначала реальность, потом Канцелярия.**
8. Final Book I movement: `Почему я?` → `Мне больно` → `Кто спрашивает?` → `Пользователь не найден`.
9. Old trilogy logic existed, but later root canon reframed the project as a cycle / serial of Chancellery cases.
10. Book II current root direction: `На всё воля Его`, with a priest / minister of undefined confession as protagonist.

## 2. Принятые решения

### 2.1 Project identity

Accepted and aligned with root canon:

- Cycle / series title: `Error 404: God Not Found`.
- Russian working title: `Ошибка 404: Бог не найден`.
- Book I title: `Почему я? / Why Me?`.
- Project is not satire against God.
- Satire targets the human tendency to turn God into a service, guarantee, contract, certificate, compensation system, ideology, emergency button, complaint authority, or support department.

### 2.2 Current external structure

Accepted and aligned with current root canon:

- The project is now a multilingual philosophical-satirical novel cycle / series.
- Older trilogy logic remains useful as internal philosophical movement / archived ore, but not as the final external structure.
- Each future book may function as a separate Chancellery case.
- Vlad opens the door, but he is not necessarily the protagonist of every future book.

### 2.3 Book I

Accepted and aligned with current root canon:

- Book I is complete as a 22-chapter draft.
- Book I should not end with enlightenment, final peace, full healing, or spiritual gloss.
- Correct final state:
  - old complaint cracked;
  - old question no longer works the same way;
  - old applicant / user is not found in the previous form;
  - the next question becomes possible: `Кто спрашивает?`.

### 2.4 Vlad

Accepted:

- Vlad is a lawyer / jurist / claimant against Creation.
- He is not a sage at the beginning and not enlightened at the end.
- He tries to formalize pain as a document.
- He wants recognition that his suffering was not meaningless and that his version is valid.
- His professional reflexes: proof, document, formulation, respondent, admissibility, procedure, recognition, status.
- His development is shown less by wisdom and more by what he no longer automatically does: he does not always grab the phone, does not always open `Личное`, does not always send from the old wound.

### 2.5 Style

Accepted:

- Main late-arc style: **балаган + философский нож**.
- First funny, then painful, then serious.
- Scene before explanation.
- Domestic precision over decorative mysticism.
- No sermons, no esoteric textbook, no clean spiritual instruction.
- Mysticism must remain impossible to prove and impossible to dismiss.
- Characters must not know they are inside a novel, chapter, story, or authorial plan.

### 2.6 Chancellery

Accepted:

- The Heavenly Chancellery is not a normal place, app, fantasy office, or magic system.
- It is a state/interface of perception.
- It manifests through the protagonist’s own language of control/search.
- For Vlad: legal-bureaucratic / digital / phone / window / category / applicant / notification.
- For the priest: second layer of thought, not screen or notification.
- It does not leave evidence fit for a complaint.
- It does not punish; it fixes / registers / sorts / redirects.

### 2.7 File naming

Accepted and aligned with current root naming rules:

- Compact storage format:

```text
E404GNFB1WM-LANG_CH-XXX_vN.ext
```

- Readable expanded names belong in indexes and chapter maps.
- Legacy readable forms like `E404_GNF_Book1_Why_Me_Glava_XX_Title_vY.ext` are useful as human labels, not necessarily current physical storage names.

## 5. Канон / вселенная

### 5.1 Core formula

Accepted:

```text
God is not “not found” because He does not exist.
God is not found because the human being searches for Him as object, address, instance, proof, justification, guarantee, or support service.

The error is not in God.
The error is in the mode of search.
```

### 5.2 Mysticism rule

Accepted:

- Mysticism remains dry, domestic, doubtful.
- It may be God, psyche, coincidence, exhaustion, interface of reality, Heavenly Chancellery, or all at once.
- Correct feeling:

```text
Доказать невозможно. Отмахнуться уже нельзя.
```

### 5.3 Ordinary reality as interface

Accepted:

- The Chancellery may highlight existing phrases rather than creating messages.
- Everyone hears the same phrase; only the one with the open inner question hears it as answer.

Accepted:

- Scripts are rhetorical templates of pain.
- They look like questions but do not seek answers; they seek fuel and repetition.

Key formula:

```text
Некоторые мысли не думаются. Они запускаются.
```

### 5.5 Box motif

Accepted:

- Chain: Donner Kebab phrase → donation box / spiritual-success packaging → home box `РАЗНОЕ` → `ПОКА НЕ ГОТОВ` → old user as box/interface.
