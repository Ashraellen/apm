# Error404 — Error 404: God Not Found

`Error404` means **Error 404: God Not Found**.

Russian title:
**Ошибка 404: Бог не найден**

Book I:
**Почему я? / Why Me?**

Short description:
A philosophical satire with mystical bureaucracy, domestic absurdity, pain under humor, and the movement from "Why me?" to "Who is asking?".

Current rule:
Do not treat the protagonist as enlightened too early. Vlad remains resistant, ironic, human, and often ridiculous in a painfully recognizable way.

Naming rule:
Core project files should use readable names first. Abbreviations may remain in dictionaries or legacy notes, but should not be the main working file names when they make the structure harder to understand.
