# Error404 — Error 404: God Not Found

`Error404` means **Error 404: God Not Found**.

Russian working title:

**Ошибка 404: Бог не найден**

Current format:

**multilingual philosophical-satirical novel cycle / series**

Book I:

**Почему я? / Why Me?**

Book II working title:

**На всё воля Его**

Possible English working title:

**On All Things His Will**

Short description:

A multilingual cycle of philosophical-satirical novels about the Heavenly Chancellery and people who try to find God, Truth, justice, meaning, or an answer — but search through distorted forms of their own fear, pain, profession, faith, knowledge, or self-deception.

Main formula:

```text
God is not “not found” because He does not exist.
God is not found because the human being searches for Him as an object, address, instance, proof, justification, or support service.

The error is not in God.
The error is in the mode of search.
```

## Chapter file naming: storage vs index

Use two layers of chapter naming.

### Storage file name

For actual stored chapter files, use the compact stable format:

```text
E404GNFB1WM-LANG_CH-XXX_vN.ext
```

Example:

```text
E404GNFB1WM-RU_CH-001_v1.docx
```

This format is good for storage, sorting, multilingual versions, and version control.

### Index / table-of-contents name

In this folder index and in chapter maps, write a readable expanded line:

```text
E404_GNF_Book1_Why_Me_Glava_01_Oshibka_404_v1.docx — Глава 1. Ошибка 404
```

The readable line is a human navigation label, not necessarily the physical storage filename.

Rule:

```text
Compact file names keep the archive clean.
Readable index names keep the author sane.
```

## Book I completed chapter index

Current completed decision: Book I has **22 chapters**.

This list describes the completed first-draft structure.  
Physical stored files may use the compact storage pattern.

```text
01. Ошибка 404
    Vlad files his first complaint against God and receives the first impossible response.

02. Окно №7
    Window №7 opens as the state of the question "Почему я?". Non-conversations reveal isolated inner monologues.

03. Демон корректных формулировок
    Respectable language hides resentment, control, fear, hatred, and self-deception.

04. Исковое состояние
    Vlad's legal/workday reality mirrors his inner attempt to turn suffering into a claim.

05. Предварительное слушание
    The complaint is checked more deeply: truth, justice, compensation, or confirmation?

06. Свеча без инструкции / earthly ritual layer
    Ordinary ritual and awkward religious form begin reflecting Vlad's need for correct access to God.

07. Архив молитв
    Vlad sees where prayers were bargaining, expectation, and attempts to manage consequences.

08. Не благодарность
    Gratitude becomes suspicious because Vlad sees invoices hidden under "thank you".

09. Счёт за добро / spiritual accounting layer
    Good deeds, suffering, correctness, reward, and resentment become part of Vlad's inner bookkeeping.

10. Радостные спасённые / Тёплый круг
    Earthly spiritual community: sincerity exists, but form and performance quickly surround it.

11. Отдел чудес
    Miracles are checked against recipient expectations. Salvation may arrive in an unacceptable form.

12. Donner Kebab
    Vlad meets ordinary enlightened people without spiritual performance. The encounter cracks his complaint.

13. Коробка
    Vlad sees that he stores proofs, scripts, and reasons not to let go.

14. Пока не готов
    The phrase becomes a real state, not an excuse.

15. Слой скриптов
    Vlad begins noticing automatic inner phrases as scripts, not truth.

16. Старый запуск
    An old script starts in an earthly situation; Vlad cannot yet stop it, but notices it.

17. Пользовательское соглашение
    Vlad begins to see the hidden terms he accepted long ago.

18. Техническая поддержка
    The Chancellery does not solve his life but shows the request behind the request.

19. Архив заявителя
    The applicant is checked through old files, memories, roles, and signatures.

20. Неверная категория
    Vlad's appeal is reclassified; the question no longer belongs where he filed it.

21. Кто спрашивает?
    The old complaint is damaged; the applicant begins to split into voices, roles, and wounds.

22. Пользователь не найден
    The old user is no longer found in the previous form. Book I ends not with enlightenment, but with a broken applicant identity.
```
