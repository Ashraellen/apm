## 4. Strong unused or partly used nodes for Book II

The following ideas are especially promising for Book II because Book II asks:

**Who is asking? / Кто спрашивает?**

### Имя не подтверждено

Possible Book II use:

Vlad sees that a name identifies a file, not necessarily the living source of a question.

Possible system language:

```text
Имя обнаружено.
Совпадение с заявителем: частичное.
```

### Биография повреждена

Possible Book II use:

Vlad's life story no longer works as stable proof of who he is.

The biography is not false, but it is not the whole subject.

Possible system language:

```text
Биография принята как материал.
Использование биографии в качестве удостоверения личности отклонено.
```

### Правота не является удостоверением личности

Possible Book II use:

Being right does not prove the one who is right.

This can become one of Book II's central blows.

Possible system language:

```text
Правота зафиксирована.
Личность не подтверждена.
```

### Представитель действует без доверенности

Possible Book II use:

Pain, resentment, fear, pride, and old scripts may act as representatives of Vlad without authorization.

Possible system language:

```text
Представитель: обида.
Доверенность: отсутствует.
Документы подписаны голосом пользователя.
```

### Авторство не подтверждено

Possible Book II use:

Vlad writes, speaks, reacts, and chooses — but later sees that not all of it came from the same center.

Possible system language:

```text
Подпись обнаружена.
Авторство не подтверждено.
```

## 5. Strong unused or partly used nodes for Book III

The following ideas are especially promising for Book III because Book III moves toward:

**Not Found / Не найден**

### Тишина без вывески

Possible Book III use:

Presence without ritual, signboard, institution, notification, or confirmation.

Not dramatic silence.
Not mystical soundtrack.
Just the place where the search for an address begins to fail.

### Укажите Бога

Possible Book III use:

The Chancellery asks Vlad to specify the object of search, and the category itself starts failing.

Possible system language:

```text
Укажите Бога.

Ошибка: указанный объект не может быть отделён от поля поиска.
```

### Присутствие обнаружено

Possible Book III use:

Not as sentimental revelation, but as technical impossibility of proving absence inside presence.

Possible system language:

```text
Присутствие обнаружено.
Объект поиска не выделен.
Запрос продолжать невозможно.
```

### Бог не найден

Possible Book III use:

Final paradox.

Not:

```text
There is no God.
```

But:

```text
God not found as object.
Search category invalid.
No external address available.
Separation protocol failed.
```

## 6. Possible future Chancellery departments

These departments may be used, transformed, or rejected later.

They should not be introduced mechanically.

### Department of Applicant Verification

Function:

Checks who is asking, who signed, who speaks, and who is being represented.

### Bureau of Damaged Biography

Function:

Shows biography as narrative evidence that may be true but incomplete.

### Registry of Unauthorized Representatives

Function:

Lists old pain, resentment, fear, pride, shame, and fatigue acting on behalf of the user.

### Archive of Roles

Function:

Stores roles Vlad used as identity: lawyer, husband, sufferer, correct person, wronged one, one who endured.

### Section of Invalid Search Categories

Function:

Book III area where searches fail because the searched-for is not an object.

### Office of Unattachable Evidence

Function:

Returns the recurring conflict: Vlad wants proof; the Chancellery gives recognition.

## 8. Future use of earthly layer

Future books should remain earthly.

Do not move the whole story into abstract Chancellery corridors.

Possible earthly mirrors for Book II:

- passport office;
- bank authorization;
- medical form;
- insurance claim;
- delivery pickup;
- legal representation;
- family conversation where different versions of Vlad answer;
- old document with outdated data;
- social media account recovery;
- forgotten password;
- two-factor authentication;
- witness statement;
- power of attorney;
- recorded voice message;
- old email written by a version of Vlad he no longer fully recognizes.

Possible earthly mirrors for Book III:

- lost address;
- map that works but leads nowhere specific;
- search engine returning everything except the thing searched;
- silence in a place without religious signs;
- ordinary conversation where no one says anything spiritual, but presence is unmistakable;
- a failed attempt to photograph or prove something that did not need proof;
- a moment where Vlad stops trying to identify the source and simply remains.

## 9. Warning against misuse

Do not treat this file as a shopping list.

A future book should not become a parade of departments.

Each idea must earn its place through:

- scene;
- conflict;
- humor;
- human contradiction;
- pressure on Vlad;
- movement of the trilogy.

The Chancellery is a precise instrument, not a decorative factory.
