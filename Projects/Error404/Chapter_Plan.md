## 5. Chapter logic

Book I should move as a path:

1. Vlad seeks the guilty party.
2. Vlad seeks God.
3. Vlad seeks himself.
4. Vlad discovers that “self,” “God,” and “world” cannot be separated in the way he was used to.

The main error is not technical.

Not simply 404.

It is the error of separation.

He searched for God as an external address.

God turned out not to be an address.

God turned out to be:
- network;
- server;
- user;
- error;
- and the one who sees the error.

## 6. Earthly and metaphysical alternation

After chapters of Heavenly support, earthly episodes may follow — or earthly episodes may trigger new offices in the Chancellery.

Possible links:

### Window №7: Why Me?

Metaphysical layer:
Vlad stands in a queue with everyone who came with the question “why me?”

Earthly layer:
He sees a queue to an icon where people came with the same question, only without a ticket number.

### Archive of Prayers

Metaphysical layer:
Vlad is shown his old prayers.

Earthly layer:
He remembers how he prayed in different places but was actually bargaining.

### Demon of Correct Formulations

Metaphysical layer:
The demon translates sins into respectable spiritual language.

Earthly layer:
Vlad hears a sermon or seminar where thirst for power is called service, fear is called calling, and course sales are called energy transfer.

### Saints on Break

Metaphysical layer:
Saints are tired of how people quote them.

Earthly layer:
Vlad sees a shop with mugs, magnets, candles, bracelets, and exclusive blessings.

### Silence Without a Signboard

Metaphysical layer:
Vlad enters the section “Request not sent.”

Earthly layer:
He first feels God where there is no signboard, ritual, instruction, cash desk, or mediator.

## 7. Intertitles and system documents

Materials once labelled “director’s cut” may be used inside the main novel as intertitles, inserts, protocols, and system logs between chapters.

Forms:
- extracts from registers;
- service notes;
- acts of Heavenly support;
- protocols checking soul algorithms;
- cases from the Department of Rejected Theophanies;
- fragments from the Catalogue of Unrecognized Theophanies;
- prayer archive records;
- system notifications;
- pseudo-official forms.

Purpose:
- strengthen previous chapter;
- prepare next chapter;
- give the novel the texture of a mystical digital document;
- connect prose with heavenly bureaucracy;
- avoid long explanations while preserving philosophical depth.

Example format:

```text
ИЗВЛЕЧЕНИЕ ИЗ РЕЕСТРА ОТДЕЛА ОТКЛОНЁННЫХ БОГОЯВЛЕНИЙ

Дело №404-7/13
Категория: "Бог явился в виде смешного"
Статус: отклонено пользователем как недостаточно возвышенное

Краткое заключение:
Пользователь ожидал знака в форме, совместимой с личными представлениями о священном.
Доставленное Богоявление было признано бытовым, несерьёзным и не соответствующим уровню духовной важности пользователя.
```

## 8. Donner Kebab placement

Donner Kebab should appear closer to the middle of the book, not at the very beginning.

It is the only major meeting with Poshpshekhon.

Function:
- earthly proof of the Law of the Seed;
- truth arrives without a signboard;
- God does not pass the dress code of Vlad’s expectations;
- the message comes through kebab, not a golden microphone.

Vlad leaves not enlightened, but touched, irritated, and offended.

Later Heavenly support says essentially the same thing in system language, and Vlad must face the fact that he had already received the answer but rejected it because it came in the wrong form.

System summary:

```text
Ранее пользователь уже получал ответ в человеческой форме.
Статус: отклонено как грубость.
Причина отклонения: отправитель не соответствовал ожиданиям пользователя о духовном авторитете.
```

## 9. Current chapter-file naming rule from v12 source

For working chapter files, the full source fixed this readable naming format:

```text
E404_GNF_Book1_Why_Me_Glava_01_Oshibka_404.md
E404_GNF_Book1_Why_Me_Glava_01_Oshibka_404.docx
E404_GNF_Book1_Why_Me_Glava_01_Oshibka_404.txt
```

For corrected versions:

```text
E404_GNF_Book1_Why_Me_Glava_01_Oshibka_404_v2.docx
E404_GNF_Book1_Why_Me_Glava_03_Demon_Korrektnyh_Formulirovok_v2.docx
```

Rules:
- chapter number always two digits: 01, 02, 03;
- chapter title in transliteration or English without spaces;
- spaces replaced by underscores;
- do not use random long variants;
- for corrected versions add `v2`, `v3` at the end.

Important note:
This readable naming rule supersedes the overly compressed later experiment `E404GNFB1WM-RU_CH-XXX_vN.ext` for user-facing working chapter files.

## 11. Practical development rule

When planning or revising chapters:

- preserve movement from complaint to recognition;
- do not over-explain the metaphysics early;
- keep Vlad resistant and human;
- balance metaphysical Chancellery with earthly scenes;
- use system documents as texture, not replacement for story;
- do not spend all strongest “director’s cut” materials too early;
- make every chapter either deepen the complaint, expose the claimant, or move toward the collapse of separation.
