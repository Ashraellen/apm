## 2. Accepted decisions

### Site positioning

Ashraellen.com should present the project not as a blog, YouTube portfolio, spiritual teaching, therapy, motivation platform, or infobusiness project, but as a multilingual artistic-philosophical research field.

The formula accepted in this chat:

> Books, videos, posts, comments, public formulas, and projects are different research instruments within one field.

### Research framing

The Research section should explain that Ashraellen’s work is not “academic scientific research” in the strict classical sense, but artistic-philosophical / literary-philosophical / practice-based research.

Preferred positioning:

> The novel is used as a research laboratory.

For the MONOLITH trilogy:

> “MONOLITH” is an artistic-philosophical study of the mechanisms through which a person loses their own perception, accepts imposed language as an inner voice, and gradually becomes part of an environment they once considered external.

The key research idea:

> I write novels as research instruments — not to entertain the reader, but to reveal in them processes that a conventional philosophical text can only describe.

### Public section framing

The Public section should not be a simple link dump to YouTube/Instagram/Telegram. It should be a public research field where thought leaves the book and meets living response.

Accepted framing:

> The public field is not a display case of activity. It is the place where thought is tested through living contact with a person.

Also accepted:

> If books show the mechanism in depth, public form records its flashes: where a thought was accepted, where it provoked resistance, where it was distorted, and where it suddenly became recognizable.

### Public formulas principle

Six selected posts are the first “public formulas” of Ashraellen. They are not merely social media posts; they are short research instruments.

Public formula cards on `/public/` must be brief but meaningful, matching the Russian master page density. They must not be only a title plus a slogan.

Standalone formula pages must be complete, not summary pages or placeholders.

### Multilingual principle

The Russian version remains the master version for meaning, density, structure, and style.

English should become the second master version, then other languages should be done manually and carefully.

The automatic generator is acceptable only for structure/scaffolding, not for final literary pages.
