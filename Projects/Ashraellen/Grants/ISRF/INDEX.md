## 1. Purpose

Institution-specific workspace for possible ISRF applications.

---

## 2. Strategic frame

Ashraellen may be adapted toward:

- independent scholarship;
- social research through literary-philosophical practice;
- interpretation and self-deception;
- language, ideology and narrative pressure;
- digital pressure and loss of agency;
- public meaning-making outside institutional academic systems.

The strongest ISRF angle is not “art project” alone, but independent cultural / social inquiry into how people interpret reality, lose agency and internalize external narratives.

---

## 3. Do not frame as

- blog;
- YouTube project;
- spiritual teaching;
- therapy;
- motivation;
- infobusiness;
- request for personal rescue.
