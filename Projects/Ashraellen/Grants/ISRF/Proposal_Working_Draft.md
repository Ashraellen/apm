# Proposal Working Draft — ISRF

Purpose:
Prepare the ISRF-specific proposal for Ashraellen.

Working frame:
Independent social research / literary-philosophical inquiry / public meaning-making / interpretation and agency.

Core argument to develop:
Ashraellen investigates how people lose agency and contact with reality when interpretation, fear, inherited scripts, ideology, language, digital pressure and social systems begin to think in their place. Literary, satirical, visual and public forms are treated as research instruments rather than content formats.

Possible structure:

1. Research question
2. Independent scholar profile
3. Method and materials
4. Social relevance
5. Existing work and evidence
6. Work plan
7. Outputs and dissemination
8. Budget and feasibility
