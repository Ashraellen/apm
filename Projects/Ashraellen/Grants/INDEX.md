# Grants — Institutional Applications

`Grants` means the institutional application workspace for the Ashraellen main ecosystem.

Purpose:
- keep each institution in its own folder;
- preserve the specific framing, requirements, drafts, budget notes, attachments and submission history;
- avoid mixing general grant strategy with ready-to-submit materials;
- make every future application reproducible instead of reconstructed from scattered chats.

Working rule:
One institution or programme = one folder.

Standard institution folder structure:

```text
INDEX.md                    — institution-specific strategy, status and file map
Application_Form_Fields.md  — known application fields and exact answers to prepare
Proposal_Working_Draft.md   — main proposal draft adapted to the institution
Budget.md                   — requested amount, budget logic and expense breakdown
Attachments_and_Links.md    — links, allowed attachments, forbidden formats, support materials
Submission_Log.md           — dates, submitted versions, responses and follow-up actions
Notes.md                    — research notes, risks, opportunities and decisions
Archive/                    — older drafts and outdated variants when needed
```

General positioning rule:
Ashraellen must not be framed as a blog, YouTube project, spiritual teaching, therapy, motivational platform, infobusiness or request for personal rescue.

Correct frame:
Independent artistic research / literary-philosophical research / multilingual public knowledge platform.

Core formula:
Ashraellen is a long-term independent literary and philosophical research project exploring self-deception, perception, inner freedom, and the human search for meaning through novels, satire, essays, video, visual formulas, and a multilingual public archive.
