# Proposal Working Draft — Kone Foundation

Purpose:
Prepare the Kone-specific proposal for Ashraellen.

Working frame:
Independent artistic research / literary-philosophical inquiry / multilingual archive.

Core argument to develop:
Ashraellen investigates how a person loses contact with reality, meaning and inner freedom through interpretation, fear, inherited scripts, language, ideology, digital pressure and self-deception. Literary, satirical, visual, audio and public forms are used as instruments of observation and recognition.

Possible structure:

1. Project summary
2. Author and method
3. Why artistic form is necessary
4. Existing body of work
5. Planned work period
6. Public and multilingual outcomes
7. Budget and feasibility
