## 2. Strategic frame

Ashraellen should be adapted toward:

- independent artistic-philosophical research;
- literature as a method of observation;
- practice-based cultural research;
- multilingual public archive;
- cultural work between languages and systems;
- long-term public research into perception, self-deception, inner freedom and the loss of contact with reality.

Kone should receive Ashraellen primarily as a serious artistic / cultural research project, not as a media platform.
