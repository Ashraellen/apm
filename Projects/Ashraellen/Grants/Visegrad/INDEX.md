## 1. Purpose

Institution-specific workspace for possible Visegrad Fund applications.

This folder should contain Visegrad-specific eligibility checks, programme research, proposal drafts, budget notes, partnership notes, attachments, links and submission log.

---

## 2. Strategic frame

Ashraellen may be adapted toward:

- Central European cultural cooperation;
- multilingual public archive;
- literature;
- public philosophy;
- migration experience;
- language borders;
- cultural translation;
- life between Belarusian / Polish / Russian-speaking / Central-Eastern European contexts;
- public cultural research across borders.

The strongest Visegrad angle is not individual authorship alone, but a cross-border cultural / literary / public knowledge project.

---

## 3. Do not frame as

- blog;
- YouTube project;
- spiritual teaching;
- therapy;
- motivation;
- infobusiness;
- request for personal rescue;
- isolated personal project without regional cooperation.
