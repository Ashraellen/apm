# Notes — John Templeton Foundation

Purpose:
Collect decisions, risks and institution-specific observations.

Current decisions:
- Treat Templeton as its own institutional workspace.
- Adapt the project toward meaning, consciousness, self-deception, inner freedom and human flourishing.
- Avoid spiritual-teaching, therapy and motivational framing.

Risks:
- Sounding too mystical.
- Sounding too literary without clear research value.
- Lacking institutional fit.
- Requesting a budget scale that does not match the programme.

Open questions:
- Best Templeton programme.
- Eligibility route.
- Budget scale.
- Need for institutional partner.
- Best formulation of the research question.
