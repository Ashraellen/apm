# Proposal Working Draft — John Templeton Foundation

Purpose:
Prepare the Templeton-specific proposal for Ashraellen.

Working frame:
Meaning, perception, self-deception, inner freedom, human flourishing, consciousness and public philosophical inquiry.

Core argument to develop:
Ashraellen investigates how human beings lose contact with reality, meaning and inner freedom when interpretation, fear, ideology, language, trauma or digital pressure begin to think in their place. The project uses novels, satire, essays, video, visual formulas and a multilingual archive as public instruments of recognition.

Important caution:
Do not make the proposal sound like a spiritual doctrine, therapy programme or motivational platform.

Possible structure:

1. Project summary
2. Central question
3. Method and public forms
4. Existing body of work
5. Why this matters for human flourishing
6. Planned work period
7. Outcomes and dissemination
8. Budget and feasibility
