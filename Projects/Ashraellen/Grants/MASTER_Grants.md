---

## 2. Core positioning

Ashraellen should be presented as:

- independent artistic research;
- literary-philosophical research;
- practice-based cultural research;
- multilingual public knowledge archive;
- long-term literary and philosophical inquiry into self-deception, perception, inner freedom and meaning;
- artistic and literary research into how contemporary human beings lose clarity, inner support and contact with reality through interpretation, information noise, external narratives and digital environments.

Avoid framing Ashraellen as:

- blog;
- YouTube project;
- spiritual teaching;
- therapy;
- motivational platform;
- infobusiness;
- media start-up;
- personal rescue request;
- content production for its own sake.

```text
Russian is the source of meaning.
Other languages are channels of access.
Translation should open the project, not rewrite its core.
```

In institutional communication, it is acceptable and important to state that the author's primary creative language is Russian and that Polish / English communication may rely on translation support for accuracy.

Preferred complex communication format: written communication. For oral conversations, the author may need translation support or questions in advance.
