# Budget — Emergent Ventures

Purpose:
Prepare the EV-specific requested amount and expense breakdown.

Working rule:
The budget must show what funding unlocks, not merely what money covers.

Budget logic:
Funding should create sustained working time and production infrastructure for completing, publishing, translating, archiving and publicly testing the Ashraellen project.

Main budget categories:

1. Living / working time
   - basic monthly support allowing the author to reduce external work and concentrate on the project;
   - this is not personal rescue, but time protection for concentrated intellectual and artistic production.

2. Production infrastructure
   - powerful laptop / workstation suitable for writing, editing, video, audio, image work and multilingual archive development;
   - monitor, storage, backup and necessary peripherals.

3. Software and services
   - AI tools;
   - transcription / translation / language support;
   - video and audio production tools;
   - hosting, domain, web infrastructure;
   - design and archive-related services.

4. Editing / translation / publication support
   - selected translation and adaptation work;
   - editorial preparation;
   - book formatting and publication costs;
   - possible external language review where necessary.

5. Website / archive development
   - multilingual archive structure;
   - professional pages;
   - research pages;
   - publication pages and long-term preservation.

EV budget tone:
Practical, lean, founder-driven. Avoid sounding like institutional overhead.
