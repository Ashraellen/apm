Working rule:
Answers must be clear, direct and self-contained. The main proposal must not depend on attachments.

## 1. Current EV application logic

Emergent Ventures asks for a compact first-stage application. The proposal must be no longer than 1500 words and must stand on its own. Supplementary materials may be uploaded, but the proposal is read first.

EV explicitly welcomes unusual and unorthodox high-reward ideas with potential for positive social change. A quick path to revenue self-sufficiency is acceptable and even treated positively.

The form states that there is no typical grant range or average award amount; funding is project-specific.

```text

```

The proposal must include:

```text
1. Personal story and why the author can build this.
2. Mainstream consensus view the author agrees with.
3. The idea and why it is new / unusual.
4. The problem being addressed.
5. What funding unlocks.
6. Budget basics.
7. Current status and 12-month acceleration path.
8. Sustainability / revenue path without sounding like infobusiness.
```
