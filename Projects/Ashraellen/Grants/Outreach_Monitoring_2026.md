## 2. General rule

Ashraellen must not be framed as:

- blog;
- YouTube project;
- spiritual teaching;
- therapy;
- motivational platform;
- infobusiness;
- request for personal rescue.

Correct frame:

```text
Independent artistic research / literary-philosophical research / practice-based cultural research / multilingual public knowledge archive.
```

Operational outreach principle:

```text
We are not asking for a grant at first contact. We establish cultural contact, test the project's readability in the relevant field, and ask for consultation, direction, dialogue, or a pointer to a suitable institution / programme / person.
```
