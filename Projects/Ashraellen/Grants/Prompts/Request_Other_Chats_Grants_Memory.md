## File names

Use the next free short name:

`GR_01.md`, `GR_02.md`, `GR_03.md`

## Collect

Collect grant-related material only:

- foundation or institution names;
- application strategy;
- accepted positioning;
- sent emails and letters;
- replies and outcomes;
- deadlines and application rules;
- budgets and eligible expenses;
- ready text fragments;
- open tasks;
- rejected or outdated ideas.
