# Mythic Method — Radiance / Сияние

This file fixes the cleaned working method for **Radiance / Сияние** after external advice was evaluated and reduced to what serves the original plan.

---

## Core genre decision

**Radiance / Сияние** is philosophical fantasy.

It should not be written defensively, as if fantasy needed to be hidden behind technological or institutional language.

The project may touch speculative fiction, mythopoetic prose and artistic research, but its living literary body is:

```text
philosophical fantasy / философское фэнтези
```

The prologue has already opened this register.

When the prologue says:

```text
В ту ночь земля молола.
```

it does not ask permission from realism, technology, or folklore commentary.

It establishes a world where mythic reality is not decoration, but an active layer of the real.

---

## Original core

The original core remains primary:

```text
Сказки содержат настоящую историю мира.
Люди называли её мифом, потому что забыли язык, на котором она была записана.
```

Book I core:

```text
Сампо — не просто потерянная мельница и не просто артефакт.
Сампо — образ живого Творения, которое кормит.
Человек стоит внутри дара, но хочет вынуть его наружу и владеть им как машиной.
```

Everything else must serve this.

If an idea makes the core more complex but less alive, remove or postpone it.

---

## What to take from external advice

External advice may be useful only as sharpening material.

It must not take the wheel.

Useful points to keep:

### 1. Do not retell the epic as plot

Radiance should not become a school retelling of **Kalevala**.

Correct method:

```text
We do not retell Kalevala.
We show the world from which such stories could have arisen.
```

The epic is not a plot outline.

The epic is a trace.

### 2. Work with functions, not decorative names

The mythic field may be recognized through living functions:

- Sampo — abundance / provision / mistaken desire to own the source;
- Song — word / voice / tuning of reality;
- Forge — form / making / responsibility for created form;
- Threshold — access / maturity / guarded resource;
- Pohjola — the cold side of desire and testing;
- Wound — damaged script mistaken for destiny;
- Forest — measure / living order / non-human law.

But these must not become schematic labels in the prose.

Characters must first be alive.

Mythic functions should be felt through action, pressure, mistakes, hunger, speech, silence, and consequence.

### 3. Let modern resistance protect the book from lecture

Nik, Thomas and other guests must prevent Ivar from becoming a smooth author-interface.

Useful dramatic rule:

```text
If everyone falls into reverent silence after Ivar speaks, the scene may be in danger.
If someone damages the solemnity and the scene becomes more alive, the scene works.
```

Ivar may open the door.

He must not become the book's explanatory machine.

### 4. Keep Sofia as the main human lens

Book I follows Sofia as the newcomer's living lens.

This supports the original plan because the reader enters the world through someone who does not yet understand it.

### 5. Do not reveal all twelve guests at once

The twelve guests must not become an immediate chart of functions.

In early chapters, keep active focus on a small number of figures:

```text
Sofia, Nik, Thomas, Eva
```

Others may remain background / chorus / pressure until the story earns their fuller emergence.

### 6. Do not make manifestations mechanical

Avoid a predictable pattern:

```text
Ivar speaks -> vision appears -> guests discuss -> repeat.
```

Strangeness should not become a scheduled attraction.

It may enter through:

- fire;
- kitchen;
- bread;
- mending;
- wet clothes;
- sleep;
- silence;
- a joke that arrives late;
- a bowl of berries;
- a sound under the floor.

---

## What to ignore or postpone

The following must not control the project:

### 1. Writing to please people who recognize the epic

The book is not an exam in Kalevala knowledge.

Readers who know the epic may recognize deeper structures.

Readers who do not know it must still receive a complete, living novel.

### 2. Over-technological language

Do not turn the literary body into terms such as:

- operators;
- engineers of reality;
- code assembly;
- micro-level reality construction;
- anomaly zones;
- protocol mechanics as surface language.

The internal term **Sampo Protocol** may remain useful for project architecture.

But the prose should mostly breathe through:

- сказка;
- земля;
- хлеб;
- голос;
- голод;
- мера;
- порог;
- дар;
- страх;
- песня;
- форма;
- живое.

### 3. Mapping every guest to a mythic function too early

Do not turn the twelve guests into a symbolic spreadsheet.

They are people first.

Each may carry hunger, fear, wound, defense, irony, fatigue or false certainty.

If they are alive, mythic echoes will appear without forcing them.

### 4. Market or institutional thinking

Do not shape the novel to fit what foreign readers, institutions, advisors, or genre categories might applaud.

The book must first become itself.

### 5. Fear of being fantasy

Do not write out of fear of being called fantasy.

This is philosophical fantasy.

The task is not to escape fantasy, but to make fantasy carry metaphysical weight.

## Clean method

The working method:

```text
Эпос не диктует сюжет.
Эпос — это след, оставшийся от настоящей истории мира.
Книга не пересказывает след, а показывает то, что могло его оставить.
```

Shorter formula:

```text
Не "Калевала в новом виде", а мир, после которого "Калевала" стала возможна.
```

---

## Book I application

Book I reveals Sampo, but in layers.

Correct movement:

```text
word -> tale -> object -> desire for a machine -> mistake of ownership -> recognition of Creation
```

Do not reveal the final meaning too early.

Do not delay the meaning so long that Book I fails to reveal Sampo.

The book's task is to let the reader first desire the machine, then fear the desire to own it, then begin to recognize the living Creation that was already feeding everyone.

---

## Chapter 2 direction after cleanup

Chapter 2 should follow the original plan, without external overcomplication.

It should not introduce technological mythology, full epic reconstruction, or symbolic diagrams.

Its function:

```text
Sampo stops being only a word and becomes a rumor / pressure / first disturbance around what feeds.
```

Chapter 2 should not yet say:

```text
Sampo is Creation.
```

Instead it should show:

- people around the fire;
- Ivar beginning a tale;
- Sofia listening without full understanding;
- Nik resisting;
- Thomas demanding clarity;
- the ground, bread, moss, cold, memory and silence beginning to answer faintly;
- ordinary reality receiving its first crack.

Working formula:

```text
Не объяснить Сампо.
Дать Сампо впервые подойти ближе.
```

---

## Final warning

If an idea makes the book cleverer but less alive, remove it.

If a scene can be described as a lecture with visual effects, rewrite it.

If the myth is present but the bread, cold, hunger, body, joke and silence disappear, the scene has lost Radiance.

Correct Radiance test:

```text
Does the scene make the reader feel that a fairy tale may have been a surviving record of reality?
```

If yes, keep going.
