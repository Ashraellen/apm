# Project_Bible — Radiance / Сияние

This file contains the core canon, metaphysics, title logic, and main philosophical architecture of **Radiance / Сияние**.

Detailed first decisions are stored in:

```text
First_Decisions.md
```

---

## 1. Project identity

Internal project folder:

```text
Radiance
```

Russian series title:

```text
Сияние
```

Series subtitle:

```text
Северные сказания о настоящей истории мира
```

Current form:

```text
philosophical speculative fiction / mythopoetic fantasy / artistic research cycle
```

The project is not a retelling of Kalevala.

It is a literary-philosophical cycle using Finnish / Karelian northern mythic material as the main spine for a broader artistic inquiry into Creation, abundance, language, craft, threshold, trauma, return, birth, forest, and human participation in the living order of the world.

---

## 2. Core premise

The ancient stories about abundance machines did not describe merely lost objects.

They preserved images of a deeper principle:

```text
Creation itself is the Manna-machine.
```

Earth already feeds.

The human being does not need to invent abundance.

The human being needs to learn how not to break the living mechanism that already feeds him.

---

## 3. Sampo

Sampo is the first entrance into the cycle.

Sampo is treated as:

- an image of abundance;
- an ancient technological-mythic symbol;
- a northern name for the principle of provision;
- a test of maturity;
- a mirror of the human desire to receive without participation;
- a fictional doorway into the deeper mechanism of Creation.

Internal term:

```text
Протокол Сампо / The Sampo Protocol
```

Public first-book title for now:

```text
Сампо
```

---

## 4. Pantheons

Pantheons are approached as functional systems, not decorative collections of gods.

Internal authorial image:

```text
Pantheons as "landing teams" — groups of higher functions arriving into human culture to teach language, craft, law, ritual, death, power, desire, order and inner transformation.
```

Institutional / cautious formulation:

```text
Pantheons as symbolic systems of transmission: mythic ensembles through which cultures encoded models of perception, craft, language, power, death, desire and inner transformation.
```

The fiction may preserve ambiguity.

It should never flatten itself into a single explanation.

---

## 5. Finnish / Karelian priority

The cycle may compare different mythic traditions, but the Finnish / Karelian / Kalevala-related line remains the main spine.

Other traditions are mirrors and echoes.

They do not replace the northern entrance.

Correct proportion:

```text
Northern spine. Comparative reflections. Philosophical depth. Literary scene.
```

---

## 6. Frame

The main frame is a campfire in a summer camp.

A strange narrator tells teenagers / almost children the "real" history of the world hidden inside northern myths.

The frame must keep the project alive and human:

- children interrupt;
- someone laughs;
- someone does not believe;
- someone asks the wrong question and opens the right door;
- the narrator refuses to become a guru;
- the fire does part of the work.

---

## 7. Series movement

Book I begins with abundance.

Then the cycle opens through functions:

```text
Сампо       — abundance / Creation as Manna-machine
Песня       — word / song / reality tuning
Кузница     — craft / form / responsibility
Порог       — access / northern power / maturity
Сбой        — trauma / damaged script / false destiny
Возвращение — death / passion / return from dissolution
Дыхание     — birth / gestation / creation without violence
Лес         — Earth / beast / living mechanism / service
```

The sequence should feel like a path of initiation, but not a manual.

---

## 8. Main danger

The main danger is explanatory heaviness.

Do not let the project become:

```text
a lecture with mythological illustrations
```

The meaning must arrive through:

- scene;
- dialogue;
- rhythm;
- contradiction;
- silence;
- recognition;
- the children's questions;
- the narrator's refusal to overexplain.

---

## 9. Working formula

```text
They searched for Sampo as a machine.
They lived inside Sampo as Creation.
They wanted provision without participation.
The Teachers came not to give them more food, but to teach them how not to destroy the food-giving world.
```
