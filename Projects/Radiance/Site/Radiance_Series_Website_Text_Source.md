## 3. What the series is about

Radiance is not a retelling of the Kalevala.

It is a literary-philosophical cycle using Finnish / Karelian northern mythopoetic material as the main spine for a broader artistic inquiry into:

```text
Creation,
abundance,
language,
craft,
threshold,
trauma,
return,
birth,
forest,
measure,
responsibility,
and human participation in the living order of the world.
```

Public website formula:

```text
Radiance asks whether ancient stories can still speak as living forms of knowledge — not as museum material, not as decorative mythology, but as image-based ways of remembering how a human being belongs to the world.
```

Russian website formula:

```text
«Сияние» спрашивает, могут ли древние истории снова говорить как живые формы знания — не как музейный материал, не как декоративная мифология, а как образный способ помнить, как человек связан с миром.
```

## 5. Mythopoetic instruction canon

Inside Radiance, ancient stories, epics, fairy tales and mythopoetic narratives are not treated as empty inventions, primitive explanations, decorative folklore or dead cultural material.

They are treated as image-based instructions.

Core formula:

```text
Fairy tales and epics are not lies about the past.
They are truth written in the language of images.
```

Russian:

```text
Сказки и эпосы — это не ложь о прошлом.
Это правда, записанная на языке образов.
```

Another formula:

```text
They are true not because they are chronicles.
They are true because they preserve law in image.
```

Russian:

```text
Они истинны не потому, что являются хроникой.
Они истинны потому, что сохраняют закон в образе.
```

Website-safe formulation:

```text
The series does not claim that every ancient story is a literal historical report. It treats mythopoetic images as carriers of deep cultural memory: forms through which human beings preserved knowledge about world-order, responsibility, measure, relation and transformation.
```

Russian website-safe formulation:

```text
Серия не утверждает, что каждая древняя история является буквальной хроникой. Она рассматривает мифопоэтические образы как носители глубокой культурной памяти: формы, через которые люди сохраняли знание о порядке мира, ответственности, мере, связи и превращении.
```

## 6. Why instruction becomes story

A direct instruction can be refused, forgotten, institutionalized, weaponized or reduced to a slogan.

A story works differently.

It passes not only through the mind, but through memory, imagination, fear, hunger, shame, longing, rhythm, compassion, recognition, desire and the ability to see oneself without being directly accused.

Core formula:

```text
We are not returning to antiquity.
We are returning the ancient image to its working force.
```

Russian:

```text
Мы не возвращаемся в древность.
Мы возвращаем древнему образу его рабочую силу.
```

Website version:

```text
Radiance does not try to turn myth into a dry system. It does not extract a diagram from the story and throw the story away. It tries to restore the force of the image itself.
```

Russian website version:

```text
«Сияние» не пытается превратить миф в сухую систему. Оно не извлекает из истории схему, чтобы выбросить саму историю. Оно пытается вернуть силу самому образу.
```

## 7. Finnish / Karelian / Kalevala-related spine

The Finnish / Karelian / Kalevala-related line is the main northern spine of the cycle.

Other traditions may appear as mirrors, echoes or comparative fields, but they do not replace the northern entrance.

Correct proportion:

```text
Northern spine.
Comparative echoes.
One deep order.
Many cultural languages.
Living literature.
```

Important public position:

```text
The Kalevala-related field is not used as decoration, costume or exotic atmosphere. It is approached as a living cultural and symbolic field through which Radiance investigates abundance, word, craft, maturity, trauma, return, birth, forest, measure and responsibility.
```

Russian:

```text
Калевальское поле не используется как украшение, костюм или экзотическая атмосфера. Оно рассматривается как живое культурное и символическое пространство, через которое «Сияние» исследует изобилие, слово, ремесло, зрелость, травму, возвращение, рождение, лес, меру и ответственность.
```

## 8. One root, many cultural languages

Alongside the Finnish / Karelian / Kalevala-related line, the project may study epics, fairy tales and ancient stories of different peoples with different degrees of depth.

The point is not to erase differences.

The point is to observe how related inner structures become visible through different cultural interfaces.

Core internal formula:

```text
Different peoples did not necessarily receive different truths.
They received different languages of access to truth.
```

Russian:

```text
Разные народы получили не разные истины, а разные языки доступа к истине.
```

Website-safe version:

```text
Different traditions are approached not as interchangeable myths, but as culturally specific languages of access to related questions of world-order, responsibility, measure, participation and transformation.
```

Russian website-safe version:

```text
Разные традиции рассматриваются не как взаимозаменяемые мифы, а как культурно особые языки доступа к родственным вопросам порядка мира, ответственности, меры, участия и превращения.
```

Avoid flattening:

```text
Do not say that all myths are the same.
Do not say that all cultures say the same thing.
Do not say that differences do not matter.
```

## 9. What Radiance is not

Radiance should not be described as:

```text
blog;
YouTube project;
spiritual teaching;
therapy;
motivation;
infobusiness;
new doctrine;
retelling of the Kalevala;
folklore reconstruction;
academic study of the epic;
content production;
fantasy using mythology as decoration;
proof that all myths are literally the same;
claim that ancient stories are literal technical manuals from God.
```

Public negative formula:

```text
Radiance is not a retelling of the Kalevala and not a reconstruction of folklore. It is a literary-philosophical artistic research cycle that asks what kind of world could give birth to such images — and what those images may still ask of us now.
```

Russian:

```text
«Сияние» — не пересказ «Калевалы» и не реконструкция фольклора. Это литературно-философский художественно-исследовательский цикл, который спрашивает, какой мир мог породить такие образы — и что эти образы всё ещё могут требовать от нас сегодня.
```

## 12. Sampo

Sampo is the first entrance into the cycle.

It is treated as:

```text
an image of abundance;
an ancient technological-mythic symbol;
a northern name for the principle of provision;
a test of maturity;
a mirror of the human desire to receive without participation;
a fictional doorway into the deeper mechanism of Creation.
```

Wrong question:

```text
Where is Sampo?
```

Deeper question:

```text
Why does the human being want to own the source while standing inside it?
```

Website version:

```text
Sampo is not only a lost object or a mythic abundance machine. In Radiance, it becomes a question about provision, ownership and participation: why does the human being seek to possess the source while already living inside the world that feeds him?
```

Russian website version:

```text
Сампо — не только утраченный предмет или мифическая машина изобилия. В «Сиянии» оно становится вопросом об обеспечении, собственности и участии: почему человек стремится владеть источником, уже находясь внутри мира, который его кормит?
```

## 13. Song

Song is not merely sound, performance, beauty or magic.

Song is an instruction about language and hearing.

Wrong question:

```text
What is the magical song?
```

Deeper question:

```text
What must change in the human being before word becomes tuning rather than noise?
```

Website version:

```text
Song continues the method of Sampo through word, hearing and relation. It asks whether speech can become more than opinion, argument or self-expression — whether language can become a form of tuning to the order of reality.
```

Russian website version:

```text
«Песня» продолжает метод «Сампо» через слово, слышание и связь. Она спрашивает, может ли речь стать чем-то большим, чем мнение, спор или самовыражение, — может ли язык стать формой настройки на порядок реальности.
```

### 15.5. “Why this series?” — English

```text
Radiance exists because ancient stories have not finished speaking. Their images may look distant, strange or impossible, but they still carry questions that modern language often fails to hold: What is abundance? What is participation? What does a word do? When does craft become responsibility? How does a damaged person return? What does it mean to live inside a world that is not an object, but a living order?

The series does not answer these questions as a lecture. It lets them become fiction.
```

### 15.6. “Зачем эта серия?” — Russian

```text
«Сияние» существует потому, что древние истории ещё не закончили говорить. Их образы могут казаться далёкими, странными или невозможными, но они всё ещё несут вопросы, которые современный язык часто не удерживает: что такое изобилие? что такое участие? что делает слово? когда ремесло становится ответственностью? как возвращается повреждённый человек? что значит жить внутри мира, который является не объектом, а живым порядком?

Серия не отвечает на эти вопросы как лекция. Она позволяет им стать художественным текстом.
```

## 17. Tone rules for website

Use:

```text
clear;
literary;
serious;
not mystical as marketing;
not academic-heavy;
not grant-bureaucratic;
not self-help;
not religious preaching;
not YouTube/content framing;
not fantasy fandom language.
```

The website should sound like:

```text
an author presenting a serious literary-philosophical cycle;
an artistic research project with public access;
a living literary world grounded in mythopoetic inquiry.
```

Avoid:

```text
I reveal the secret truth of all myths.
I explain the real Kalevala.
My books prove the hidden history of the world.
This is spiritual teaching.
This is therapy.
This is a new doctrine.
This is a fantasy universe based on mythology.
```

Better:

```text
The series asks.
The fiction investigates.
The ancient image is treated as a living carrier of memory.
The project searches for responsible forms of access to old stories.
```
