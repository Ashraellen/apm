# Institutions — Radiance / Сияние

This folder will store notes, drafts, replies and contact logic for Finnish / cultural / research institutions connected to **Radiance / Сияние**.

---

## First-contact principle

Do not begin with a grant request.

Begin with research orientation:

```text
I am developing a philosophical speculative fiction / artistic research project involving Kalevala-related material and would like guidance regarding sources, context, responsible use of the material, and possible expert direction.
```

---

## Possible future contacts

```text
Kalevala Society
Finnish Literature Society / SKS
Uniarts Helsinki / artistic research networks
other relevant Finnish / Karelian cultural or research bodies
```

---

## Future grant bridge

The institutional line may later support a working-time grant application.

But the first step is cultural and research legitimacy, not immediate funding.
