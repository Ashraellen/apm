# Sampo_Concise_Research_Note_Plan

Status: ACTIVE PLANNING NOTE  
Created: 2026-06-28  
Updated: 2026-06-28

This file records the decision to prepare a short conceptual companion text for **Radiance. Book I: Sampo**.

---

## 1. Decision

A short "treatise-like" document is useful and allowed, but it must not replace the novel or become the primary form of the project.

Its correct role:

```text
The novel is the living artistic body of the research.
The concise research note is the map / key / skeleton of what the novel investigates.
```

Russian working formula:

```text
Роман — живое художественное тело исследования.
Краткий трактат / записка — сжатая карта того, что было исследовано.
```

---

## 2. Language order decision

The first working version of the concise research note must be written in **Russian**.

Reason:

```text
The author is Russian-speaking.
The primary creative and conceptual language of Radiance / Сияние is Russian.
The method and dossier already state that Russian is the source language of the work.
The English version should be a later transcreation / adaptation, not the place where the core thought is first invented.
```

Correct workflow:

```text
1. Russian source note
2. Author review / correction in Russian
3. English transcreation / Kone-facing adaptation
4. Optional Polish orientation note if needed later
```

Russian target file:

```text
Projects/Radiance/Research/Sampo/Sampo_Concise_Research_Note_RU.md
```

Future English target file:

```text
Projects/Radiance/Research/Sampo/Sampo_Concise_Research_Note_EN.md
```

Do not start from English if the task is to define the actual inner meaning of the research.

## 3. Preferred name

For internal use, the author may call it a short treatise.

Russian working title:

```text
Сияние: Сампо
Краткая литературно-философская записка об изобилии и участии
```

Alternative Russian title:

```text
Сампо
Краткий трактат об изобилии и участии
```

For grants, institutions, editors and public-facing material in English, prefer:

```text
Radiance: Sampo
A Concise Literary-Philosophical Research Note
```

Alternative English titles:

```text
Sampo: A Concise Research Note
Sampo: A Literary-Philosophical Research Note
Sampo: A Concise Treatise on Abundance and Participation
```

Avoid making **treatise** the main public label unless the context specifically asks for such a form.

Reason:

```text
Treatise sounds heavy and may contradict the core frame that Radiance transforms research into living literature.
Research Note / Companion Note sounds clearer, lighter and more grant-compatible.
```

---

## 4. Function

The concise note should:

- explain the research question behind **Sampo**;
- show that the novel is a completed artistic-research outcome;
- extract the philosophical skeleton without killing the literary body;
- help grant readers, editors, translators and partners understand the method quickly;
- prevent the book from being mistaken for decorative fantasy, folklore retelling or a vague spiritual text.

It should not:

- retell the plot chapter by chapter;
- explain every symbol;
- turn the novel into a lecture;
- replace the novel;
- pretend to be an academic paper on Kalevala.

---

## 5. Recommended length

```text
5–8 pages
maximum 10 pages
```

The note must be short enough to be read before or after a sample excerpt.

## 7. Core formulas to preserve

```text
Sampo cannot be taken as a thing.
Abundance cannot be separated from participation.
The world already feeds the human being.
The human being wants to pull the source out and call it his own.
The error is not hunger itself, but the desire for provision without participation.
```

Ошибка не в голоде, а в желании обеспечения без участия.

## 8. Russian draft opening seed

```text
Сампо в этой работе рассматривается не как потерянный мифологический предмет и не как устройство, которое нужно восстановить, объяснить или присвоить. Сампо рассматривается как литературно-философский мотив, через который Книга I исследует изобилие, владение, голод, участие и узнавание.

Главный вопрос Книги I прост: почему человек ищет машину изобилия, стоя внутри живого мира, который уже его кормит?

Роман отвечает на этот вопрос не через доказательство, а через художественную форму. Хлеб, вода, земля, дом, голод, стыд, забота, молчание и узнавание становятся инструментами исследования. Результат не является теоретической системой, добавленной к роману извне. Сам роман является формой исследования.
```

## 9. English draft opening seed for later

```text
Sampo is not treated here as a lost mythological object, nor as a device to be reconstructed, explained or possessed. It is approached as a literary-philosophical motif through which the book investigates abundance, ownership, hunger, participation and recognition.

The central question of Book I is simple: why does the human being search for a machine of abundance while standing inside a living world that already feeds him?

The novel answers this question not through argument, but through fiction. Bread, water, soil, house, hunger, shame, care, silence and recognition become the instruments of inquiry. The result is not a theoretical system added to the novel from outside. The novel itself is the research form.
```
