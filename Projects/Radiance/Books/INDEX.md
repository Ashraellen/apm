## 6. Series research purpose

Radiance is not an attempt to explain Kalevala through a modern lens.

Correct purpose:

```text
To investigate the human being and reality through the mythopoetic traces in which epic preserved knowledge about the human being and reality.
```

For Finnish / Karelian / Kalevala-related contexts:

```text
The project approaches the Kalevala field not as a retelling target, but as a living cultural trace through which literary artistic research can explore inner laws of abundance, language, craft, threshold, trauma, death, birth, forest, measure, responsibility and participation.
```
