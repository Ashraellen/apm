# Season_Setting — Book I: Сампо

Working seasonal decision for **Сияние. Книга I. Сампо**.

Date fixed: 2026-06-13

---

## Core seasonal decision

The modern frame of Book I takes place at the end of summer and the beginning of autumn.

Working formula:

```text
late August / early September
northern Finland or another northern forest-water region
not white nights
not deep winter
not postcard Christmas North
```

---

## Why this season works

Book I is about abundance, feeding, Earth, provision, participation, hunger, ownership and recognition.

The land should still be visibly feeding:

- berries;
- mushrooms;
- fish;
- water;
- bread;
- damp moss;
- grass still alive;
- cold air;
- rot and growth at the same time.

This is not the dead purity of winter.

This is the border where the world still gives openly, but already begins to withdraw its face.

---

## White nights rule

Do not set the modern frame during full white nights.

Reason:

```text
White nights are beautiful, but they weaken the fire as threshold.
```

If night does not truly arrive, the campfire risks becoming decoration.

Book I needs the fire to become a real first door.

---

## Northern lights rule

Northern lights may appear, but only as a rare sign.

They must not become tourist decoration or constant atmosphere.

Current rule:

```text
The prologue may use northern lights as mythic pressure.
The modern frame should not repeat them too early.
A weak green reflection or later aurora may appear only if earned by the story.
```

The aurora should function as confirmation / disturbance / sign, not as a postcard.

---

## Prologue vs modern frame

The prologue may stand in winter:

```text
snow
frozen lake
northern lights
Earth grinding under the white crust
```

The modern frame stands in late northern summer / early autumn:

```text
wet moss
cool evenings
returning darkness
berries and mushrooms
lake water
simple food
fire becoming possible as threshold
```

This contrast is important.

In the prologue, people try to pull Sampo from winter depth.

In the modern frame, the guests stand among late abundance and still do not immediately recognize that Sampo is already around them.

---

## Atmosphere

The dominant atmosphere:

```text
cooling abundance
wet forest
returning night
late light
moss, lake, bread, smoke, damp wood, mushrooms, berries
```

The season should feel like a threshold:

```text
summer has not fully left
winter has not arrived
the world is still feeding
but the withdrawal has begun
```

This supports the main movement of Book I:

```text
from Sampo as object
through Sampo as abundance machine
into Sampo as Creation itself
```

---

## Working image

Book I should feel seasonal like cold northern butter:

```text
cold butter first resists the knife,
then lies on hot bread,
and the reader understands that he is already eating what he wanted to explain.
```

This is the desired texture of the book.

Not a story about feeding.

A book that itself begins to feed.
