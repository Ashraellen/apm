## Transcreation approach

The English draft was created as literary transcreation preserving the chapter’s movement from the dry white place on the map into the wet, full, resistant physical place in the forest. The key pressure is that the real hollow does not match the map and does not become an answer, not because it is empty, but because it is already full of its own life, water, moss, decay, insects, mushrooms, leaves, and work.

The chapter continues the map/trace logic but reverses the relief of the scheme: the place refuses reduction. The map wants correction; the place simply is.

## Key choices

- **Title:** `Глава восьмая. Место` rendered as **Chapter Eight. The Place**.
- `пойдём искать` rendered as **“let’s go look”** rather than “let’s go search” to preserve the denial around looking/searching.
- `Слова стояли рядом... как мокрые сапоги` rendered as **“The words stood beside them like wet boots by the door.”** Keeps the physicalization of unspoken intention.
- `карта лежала в закрытом блокноте` rendered as **“The map lay in the closed notebook.”** Simple and exact.
- `он слишком аккуратно не смотрел` rendered as **“He was not looking too carefully.”** Keeps the paradox of intentional non-looking.
- `нож оказался рядом, и рука сделала то, что могла сделать без карты` rendered as **“the knife was nearby, and the hand did what it could do without a map.”** Preserves hand/action logic.
- `самое оно` style around naming Sampo: **“Now it had been named almost as the thing they were not doing.”** This keeps the denial pressure.
- `ничего не ищем вслух` rendered as **“we are not looking for anything aloud.”** Keeps Nick’s precise irony.
- `Тем, кто хочет закрыть вопрос, лучше идти медленно` rendered as **“Those who want to close a question had better walk slowly.”** Central Ivar line; preserved directly.
- `решение приняли без решения` rendered as **“The decision was made without a decision.”** Kept as an authorial pressure line, not external meta.
- `День был светлее, чем вчера, но не добрее` rendered as **“The day was lighter than yesterday, but not kinder.”** Direct and strong.
- `просто мокрое место` rendered as **“simply a wet place.”** Preserves Nick/David/Marek conflict.
- `философ подошв` rendered as **“philosopher of soles.”** Maintains the running Marek/feet humor.
- `мох медленно прощает` rendered as **“moss forgives slowly.”** Strange but source-faithful.
- `влажность` / `двери открыты` joke preserved as **“the doors are open... judging by the humidity, they have been for a long time.”**
- `каждый шаг требовал внимания. Не внимания к смыслу. К ноге.` rendered as **“every step required attention. Not attention to meaning. To the foot.”** Preserves bodily anti-interpretation.
- `место не имело обязанности совпадать` rendered as **“the place had no obligation to match.”** Central line.
- `Вот. Место.` rendered as **“Here. / The place.”** Marek’s dry declaration preserved.
- `место было` rendered as **“The place was.”** This is central and deliberately stark.
- `не точка / не знак / не пустота` rendered as **“Not a point. / Not a sign. / Not an emptiness.”**
- `будущая земля` rendered as **“future earth.”** Preserves decay/work logic.
- `не смертью. Работой.` rendered as **“Not death. Work.”** Exact and important.
- `карта уже держала его взгляд за ворот` rendered as **“the map was already holding his gaze by the collar.”** Preserves bodily coercion.
- Ivar’s line `Здесь уже было здесь` rendered as **“Here was already here.”** Strange but necessary.
- `Ничего для карты` rendered as **“Nothing for the map.”** Minimal and Ivar-like.
- `Это не география` rendered as **“That is not geography.”** Preserves David’s inability to accept Marek’s place-logic.
- `Вот оно` rendered as **“There it was.”** Marks the next-step mechanism.
- `Это ответ места` rendered as **“That is the place’s answer.”** Central Marek line.
- `Выбирайте, что вам нужно доказать` rendered as **“Choose what you need to prove.”** Keeps Marek’s severe practicality.
- `Он хотел закрыть` rendered as **“He wanted to close.”** Keeps David’s closure-drive.
- `Там тоже будет место` rendered as **“There will be a place there too.”** One of the chapter’s core lines.
- `Он ждал, пока боль сама перестанет держаться за шляпку` rendered as **“He waited until the pain itself stopped holding on to the head.”** Chosen to preserve nail imagery and strangeness.
- `низина была не белой` rendered as **“the hollow was not white.”** Maintains map/place contrast.
- `место не прошло проверку` rendered as **“The place has failed inspection.”** Keeps Nick’s procedural irony.
- `следователь / подозреваемый` not explicit in Russian, but `назначили подозреваемой` rendered as **“appointed as a suspect.”** Retains legal/investigative joke.
- `дом после низины... скорее суше` rendered as **“after the hollow the house looked different. Not safer. Rather drier.”** Keeps the dry/safe distinction.
- `хотя бы физически` rendered as **“Then at least physically.”** Marek’s dry line preserved.
- `Можно исправить?` rendered as **“May I correct it?”** Keeps the strange permission question.
- `Потому что ты уже знаешь, что это не закроет` rendered as **“Because you already know it will not close anything.”** Central Ivar correction.
- `На сегодня` rendered as **“For today.”** Preserved as the merciful unit of time.
- Final: **“It had been full. / It simply had not been their answer.”** Exact final pressure.

## Meta-trace audit watchpoints

- No unauthorized external `chapter`, `scene`, `page`, `author`, `reader`, `novel`, `plot`, `character`, `ending`, `prologue`, or `epilogue` language was added.
- Terms like `section`, `inspection`, `suspect`, `document`, `correct`, and `map` are internal to the group’s map/investigation logic.
- The abstraction remains inside Sofia’s perception and the shared drift from map to place.
- Ivar remains action-based and minimal; he does not explain the chapter’s meaning.

## Known risks

- **Volume:** preserved; no intentional compression.
- **Humor:** preserved through Nick, Marek, Nora, and Sofia; humor remains under pressure.
- **Sampo canon:** preserved. Sampo is named as the thing they are “not” looking for, but not found, explained, or located.
- **Ivar rule:** preserved. Ivar walks behind, says little, and refuses to close or forbid the map correction.
- **Place logic:** preserved. The hollow is not a symbol and not an answer. It is full of details and its own life.
- **English smoothness:** several lines remain intentionally strange: `Here was already here`, `Nothing for the map`, `the place had no obligation to match`, `the map was holding his gaze by the collar`, `the hollow was not white`.
