## Transcreation approach

The English draft was created as literary transcreation preserving the chapter’s movement from the hollow and the map back into the body, shame, food, nausea, ants, earth-work, and the return of what a human being would prefer to call unnecessary. The key is that return is not moralized or beautified: the body refuses the map, shame enters the kitchen, ants work without knowing the human meaning, and Sofia refuses to turn this too quickly into a “lesson” or “cycle of life.”

The chapter’s pressure is bodily and domestic: soup, infusion, stomach, vomit, water, bowl, someone else’s sweater, cold air, ants, spoiled porridge, bread, crumbs, and the notebook still present but displaced.

## Key choices

- **Title:** `Глава девятая. Возврат` rendered as **Chapter Nine. Return**.
- `дом кормил их слишком старательно` rendered as **“the house fed them too diligently.”** Preserves living-house pressure.
- `как чистая скатерть на болоте` rendered as **“like a clean tablecloth on a bog.”** Direct and stark.
- `всё это постепенно сложилось в ужин` rendered as **“all of it gradually assembled itself into dinner.”** Keeps the self-assembling domestic movement.
- `обязательный` rendered as **obligatory**, not compulsory, to preserve bodily necessity.
- `инструмент для северного вечера` rendered as **“another tool for a northern evening.”** Keeps Marek’s practical-object logic.
- `лекарство / для согрева / вкус` preserved as **medicine / for warming up / taste**.
- `держал ложку слишком ровно` rendered as **“held his spoon too evenly.”** Keeps bodily tension.
- `Карта снова была в доме. Как и они.` rendered as **“The map was back in the house. / So were they.”**
- `низина входила частями` rendered as **“It entered in pieces.”** Central line.
- David eating bread to put something dense inside: rendered as **“place something dense inside himself, so that it would press down what would not close.”**
- `масло... сдавалось сразу` rendered as **“The butter... surrendered at once.”** Preserves animate/object pressure.
- Nick’s `древний северный приговор` rendered as **“an ancient northern sentence.”** Keeps sentence/judgment ambiguity.
- Ivar eating soup without symbolic framing preserved exactly in function.
- `подъём... попросил политического убежища` rendered as **“requested political asylum.”** Preserves Nick’s absurd bureaucratic humor.
- `поверхность, по которой можно было провести линию и ошибиться` rendered as **“a surface on which one could draw a line and make a mistake.”** Maintains map contamination.
- `еда казалась... обязанностью перед телом` rendered as **“food seemed... an obligation to the body.”** Central bodily pivot.

- Final ant scene preserved: not relief, not cleanliness, not beauty, not gratitude to life-cycle; simply looking.
- Final line: **“this was unnecessary only for a human being.”** preserves `лишним это было только для человека`.

## Meta-trace audit watchpoints

- No unauthorized external `chapter`, `scene`, `page`, `author`, `reader`, `novel`, `plot`, `character`, `ending`, `prologue`, or `epilogue` language was added.
- Terms like `event`, `material`, `symbolism`, `meaning`, `answer`, `work`, and `return` are internal to Sofia’s perception and the bodily/domestic logic.
- The chapter avoids beautifying the ants and decomposition as a spiritual lesson; this is important.
- Sampo remains not solved, not named as doctrine, not reduced to ecological cycle; Sofia almost hears the word as a too-large circle but resists beautifying it.

---

## Known risks

- **Volume:** preserved; no intentional compression.
- **Humor:** preserved but muted after the bodily crisis. Nick’s restraint is important.
- **Bodily shame:** preserved dryly, without sensationalizing or euphemizing too much.
- **Sampo canon:** preserved. Sampo is almost heard as a too-large circle, but not explained.
- **Ivar rule:** preserved. Ivar acts minimally, gives only necessary direction, and does not convert David’s humiliation into teaching.
- **English smoothness:** several lines remain intentionally strange: `different works`, `returning direction`, `ordinary humiliation`, `the earth does not skip material`, `the world was busy`, `You will look who wants it`.
