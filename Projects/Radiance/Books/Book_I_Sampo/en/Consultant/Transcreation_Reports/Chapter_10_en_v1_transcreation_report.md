## Transcreation approach

The English draft was created as literary transcreation preserving the chapter’s shift from return/body/shame into share, portion, invisible accounting, labour, bread, water, firewood, notebook ownership, and the danger of dividing Sampo before Sampo has even been found.

The central pressure is that no one openly holds a meeting and no one openly starts a ledger, yet the house begins counting: water, bread, berries, work, fatigue, the right to sit, the right not to carry the bucket, the right to a larger or smaller piece, and eventually the right to say “mine” or “ours.”

## Key choices

- **Title:** `Глава десятая. Доля` rendered as **Chapter Ten. Share**.
- `доля` rendered primarily as **share**, not `portion` or `lot`, to preserve both allocation and participation. The word also retains a faint tension with fate/portion, but the house-table level remains primary.
- `дом стал считать сам` rendered as **“the house began counting by itself.”** Preserves living-house pressure and invisible accounting.
- `количество вошло в комнаты раньше людей` rendered as **“quantity entered the rooms before the people did.”** Keeps abstraction entering as physical presence.
- `говорить мелкими` rendered as **“speaking in small ones.”** Retains the shift from large words to small practical words.
- Marek’s bucket line `Оно молчит. Поэтому надёжнее большинства.` rendered as **“It is silent. That makes it more reliable than most.”** Preserves dry northern pragmatism.
- `Так бывает после стыда...` rendered as **“It happens that way after shame: the body returns before the person permits himself to return.”** Important continuation from Chapter 09.
- `Кто-то не идёт. / Кто-то вместо него идёт.` rendered as **“Someone does not go. / Someone goes instead of him.”** Keeps the count emerging from absence and substitution.
- `отложенным движением` rendered as **postponed movement**. Strong Thomas formulation.
- Marek’s `Чтобы вода вернулась` rendered as **“So the water comes back.”** Simple, severe, and central.
- `кухня снова стала домом. / Но уже не совсем.` rendered as **“the kitchen became a house again. / But not entirely.”**
- `Можно ли устать от того, что просто увидел?` rendered as **“Can a person grow tired from something he merely saw?”**
- Lina’s work logic rendered as: **“if one begins weighing every work, half of what is necessary will have to stop being done.”**
- Nick’s state humour preserved through **birth of the state**, **organs of administration**, **labour relations**, **butter tax**, **state labour**, **working class**, and **taxes**.
- `чтобы не дать количеству стать лицом` rendered as **“so as not to let quantity acquire a face.”** Key Nick function.
- `когда мало, рука начинает знать о себе больше, чем хочется` rendered as **“when there is little, the hand begins to know about itself more than one would like.”** Central bodily/accounting line.
- `доля таланта` rendered as **“my share of talent.”** Allows the word `share` to become charged.
- `Сколько взять...` sequence kept as **How much...** repetitions.
- `ноги сами попросили` rendered as **“her legs asked by themselves.”** Preserves body acting before thought.
- David’s uselessness line preserved, with Nick’s pride line: **“It is one of the most exhausting forms of pride.”**
- Nora’s refusal of `useless` preserved as simple dislike of the word, without saving everyone.
- `Вода вернулась` rendered as **“The water has returned.”** Echoes Chapter 09 title/return logic.
- `уже сходили` rendered as **“we already went.”** The invisible count begins in an ordinary phrase.
- `кто-то уже внёс` rendered as **“Someone already contributed.”** Keeps contribution/accounting register.
- Ivar stripping bark kept concrete; Nick’s `meaning starts climbing out by itself` preserves the danger of over-reading.
- `она стала тем, чего должно хватить` rendered as **“It became that which had to be enough.”** Central bread transformation.
- `дрожжи ... честно поднимают` rendered as **“if it is alive, it rises honestly.”** Preserves Marek’s dry literal/deep register.
- `маленькую жадность...` rendered as **“a small greed, a small fatigue, a small unwillingness to carry the bucket.”**
- David’s notebook line: `Я не карту` rendered as **“Not the map.”** Simple, exact, and socially loaded.
- `не для счёта` rendered as **“Not for keeping score.”** Preserves David’s denial.
- `честно` sequence preserved as **Honestly to...** repetitions.
- `как близко честность стоит к счёту` rendered as **“how close honesty stands to counting.”** Central conceptual hinge.
- Ivar: `Это уже не дар. Это кусок.` rendered as **“This is no longer a gift. It is a piece.”** Key line.
- `Кусок умеет быть моим. / Дар — хуже.` rendered as **“A piece knows how to be mine. / A gift is worse.”** Strange, but source-faithful and central.
- Bread/power sequence preserved: cutting as political question, power arising from bread.
- `щедрость... как место для себя` rendered as **“generosity that a person can also use as a place for himself.”** Important ethical ambiguity.
- `не всем вчера было плохо` rendered as **“Not everyone was sick yesterday.”** The avoided phrase returning as care/accounting.
- `помощь тоже может положить человека в отдельную стопку` rendered as **“help too can put a person in a separate stack.”** Important line; controller should review `stack`.
- `участие через хлеб тоже имело размер` rendered as **“participation through bread also had a size.”**
- Firewood sorting preserved as another share/order system: now/later, dry/wet, good/wait.
- `В мой?` / `В наш` rendered as **“In mine?” / “In ours.”** Ownership pivot preserved.
- `у тревоги тоже может появиться владелец` rendered as **“even anxiety can acquire an owner.”** Central line.
- Ancient glint/vision: **unfound object**, rumour, possible place, possible road, full bowl, distribution before appearance. Kept as brief, splinter-like, not grand.
- `делили то, чего не было` rendered as **“They were dividing what wasn’t there.”**
- David’s notebook claim preserved clearly: **“It is simply my notebook. I need at least something to be simply mine...”**
- Nick’s **respiratory accounting** preserves comic bureaucratic absurdity.
- Ivar’s `Дрова` rendered as **Firewood**: one-word return to body.
- Notebook/map ownership resolution preserved: notebook David’s, map common if he wants, but it remains in Thomas’s head anyway.
- Berries/share scene preserved: Nora divides, Nick excluded after refusal, David receives a sour spoonful, and explanation is intentionally short.
- `Иногда дар можно спасти...` rendered as **“Sometimes a gift can be saved only by not explaining it for too long.”**
- Final table inventory preserved: everything common / almost everything could become someone’s.
- `слово было произнесено. Доля появилась. Не как закон. Как ощущение в руке.` rendered as **“the word had been spoken. Share had appeared. Not as law. As a sensation in the hand.”**
- Sofia’s bread piece rendered as **“a small, temporary, edible boundary between ‘there is’ and ‘mine.’”**
- Final: **“They had found nothing yet. / But they had already begun to divide.”** Preserves the chapter’s closing blow.

## Meta-trace audit watchpoints

- No unauthorized external `chapter`, `scene`, `page`, `author`, `reader`, `novel`, `plot`, `character`, `ending`, `prologue`, or `epilogue` language was added.
- Words like `report`, `state`, `administration`, `labour`, `tax`, `accounting`, `ownership`, `private ownership`, `unauthorized copying`, and `count` are internal to Nick’s bureaucratic irony and the chapter’s share/counting logic.
- The glint/vision is kept as Sofia’s brief perception, not as an external explanatory flashback.
- Ivar remains minimal and object/action based. His key lines are attached to bread, piece, firewood, knife, and body.

---

## Known risks

- **Title:** `Share` may feel abstract in English, but it preserves the chapter’s logic better than `Portion` alone.
- **Humor:** bureaucratic/state humour is dense; preserved deliberately.
- **Objectness:** bread, bucket, water, firewood, berries, notebook, knife, towel, gloves all remain concrete and active.
- **Sampo canon:** preserved. Sampo is not found or explained; the danger is that division begins before the object appears.
- **English smoothness:** several lines remain intentionally strange: `quantity entered`, `speaking in small ones`, `A piece knows how to be mine`, `a gift is worse`, `Share had appeared... as a sensation in the hand`.
