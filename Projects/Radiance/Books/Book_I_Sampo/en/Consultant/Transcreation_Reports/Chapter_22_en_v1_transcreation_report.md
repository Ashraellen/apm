## 1. Chapter function preserved

Chapter 22 follows participation with recognition. It does not solve `Sampo`; it changes the way the word stands among things.

The chapter movement is preserved:

1. Rain stops, but wetness remains.
2. Sofia wakes with the body sorting yesterday’s work into parts.
3. Dirt under the nail becomes no longer dirt, but trace.
4. The ditch still holds; `for now` becomes a state of things, not refusal.
5. Sofia goes outside not to check, but because the hand remembers better than the head.
6. Porch, fish scale, mud, ditch, board, stones, glove, barrel, path to the lake: objects become linked without becoming an answer.
7. Nick’s mismatched socks keep the air from becoming solemn.
8. David spreads out all his papers, then does not write.
9. Old maps/recipes/notes are no longer ridiculous; they contain effort.
10. Thomas sees the map is smaller than what has happened, but not rubbish.
11. Marek’s response to `what can one count on`: not on everything, but on what one does when it goes the wrong way.
12. `Almost` becomes kin to `for now`.
13. Sofia refuses to connect papers/words correctly too soon.
14. The `SAMPO? / Сампо?` sheet is placed among other sheets and begins to look strange because it stands alone.
15. Final recognition: Sofia cannot understand why `Sampo` is one.

No structural changes recommended.
