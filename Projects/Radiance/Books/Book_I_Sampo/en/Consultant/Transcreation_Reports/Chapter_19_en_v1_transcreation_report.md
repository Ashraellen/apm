## 1. Chapter function preserved

Chapter 19 moves from exchange/place to lack. The previous chapter taught that nothing simply disappears; Chapter 19 tests this by making something actually disappear from the human side of the evening: the fish is taken by boars, berries are scattered, mushrooms are crushed.

The chapter preserves the movement:

1. The house rests after lunch.
2. Marek proposes not “to gather” or “to search,” but “to look.”
3. The forest offers a little: mushrooms, sour berries, one fish.
4. The basket becomes a modest promise.
5. “Enough” appears as bodily relief.
6. The basket is left on the porch.
7. The boars take the fish and damage the rest.
8. The characters confront lack, shame, anger, and the uselessness of “if we had.”
9. Lina turns loss into work with what remains.
10. Final object image: three mushrooms and fish scales in the basket; the rest remains outside.

No structural changes recommended.

## 2. Title decision

Russian:

```text
Глава девятнадцатая. Недостаток
```

Chosen English:

```text
Chapter Nineteen. Lack
```

Rationale:

`Lack` is plain, hard, and direct. Alternatives:

- `Shortage` would sound logistical.
- `Insufficiency` would be too abstract.
- `Not Enough` would over-explain the emotional center.

`Lack` preserves the material and emotional pressure: not enough food, not enough foresight, not enough joke, not enough air, not enough of themselves.

Recommendation: keep `Lack`.

## 3. Central chapter pressure

Key pressure:

```text
Enough can become not enough after the body has already believed in it.
```

This is preserved through the basket:

- first empty;
- then one mushroom;
- then five mushrooms;
- then berries;
- then fish;
- then supper;
- then overturned basket;
- then three mushrooms and fish scales.

The emotional damage is not just hunger; it is the loss of an imagined supper.

## 5. Character voice notes

### Sofia

Sofia’s key arc is the bodily belief in enough, then the shame of being angry at a loss before it was even fully hers. She does not moralize; she watches the rain make the broken mushroom smaller.

### Marek

Marek remains spare and exact:

- `Usually is not a contract.`
- `There is one.`
- `I don’t know what will close.`
- `That is why we checked.`
- `Because we didn’t.`
- `Find the place where it is already gone.`

He is not wise as performance; he is practical under changing conditions.

### Thomas

Thomas’s need for measure meets lack. Important growth: he does not explode. He says he does not like lessons appearing after mistakes. He begins to understand post-fact knowledge as painful but real.

### Nick

Nick jokes less and quieter. His silences matter. Two times he does not joke when expected. This should be preserved.

### Lina

Lina turns loss into work. `All right` begins sorting. She does not comfort by denial. She says: `It is part of supper.`

### Eva

Eva keeps the world in action: hands, help, washing, practical assignments. She smiles first only near the end after Nick’s boar line.

### David

David’s notebook remains mostly closed. His key line is `I had already counted it differently.` He resists turning the loss into a scheme immediately.

### Nora

Nora stays in the house; this is an important choice. No need to force her into the outdoor search scene.
