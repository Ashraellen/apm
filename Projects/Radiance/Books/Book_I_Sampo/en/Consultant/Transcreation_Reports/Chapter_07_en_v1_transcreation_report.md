## Transcreation approach

The English draft was created as literary transcreation preserving the chapter’s shift from trace/evidence into map, scheme, arrangement, white space, reduction, and the relief of seeing disorder become dry lines on paper. The chapter does not make the map a mystical object; rather, it shows how the act of mapping reduces the forest, and how the white place on paper becomes another form of waiting emptiness.

The central pressure is the difference between the wet forest and the dry page: roots, boards, mud, moss, moose track, wet clothes, distance measured by exhaustion — all are reduced into lines, dots, gaps, and a small white space that begins to ask for a question.

## Key choices

- **Title:** `Глава седьмая. Карта` rendered as **Chapter Seven. The Map**.
- `карта` rendered as **map** throughout.
- `схема` rendered as **diagram** in David’s direct line and **scheme** at the end. This preserves the distinction between a map and a simplified operative pattern.
- `дом высох не до конца / сделал вид, что справился` rendered as **“the house had not dried completely / It had only pretended it had coped.”** Keeps the living-house pressure.
- `последним официальным институтом здравого смысла` rendered as **“the last official institution of common sense.”** Preserves Nick’s comic bureaucratic register.
- `географического отчёта` rendered as **“geographical report.”** Kept dry and comic.
- `расположены` rendered as **arranged**, preserving the dangerous shift from place-listing to relation/structure.
- `можно соединить` rendered as **“it can be connected.”** Core chapter hinge.
- `Вот так погибают вечера` rendered as **“This is how evenings die.”** Preserves Nick’s dry fatalism.
- `Бумаги — нет. Того, что люди на ней делают, — да.` rendered as **“Not of paper. Of what people do on it — yes.”** Preserves the distinction.
- `карта чего? Тропы. Места.` rendered as **“A map of what? / The path. / The place.”** Preserves the mismatch of David/Thomas.
- `На карте не мокро` rendered as **“On a map it is not wet.”** Central Ivar line, kept exact and simple.
- `лесу выдали формат` rendered as **“The forest has simply been issued a format.”** Keeps Nick’s bureaucratic irony.
- `лес входит в карандаш` rendered as **“the forest enter the pencil.”** Strange but central; preserves the reduction process.
- `лось посмертно назначен сотрудником навигации` rendered as **“The moose has been posthumously appointed an employee of navigation.”** Preserves Nick’s absurd-bureaucratic humor.
- `Каждая вещь на бумаге получала место...` rendered as **“Every thing on the paper received a place. Every place — a name. Every name — the possibility of being connected with another.”** Preserves rhythm and logic.
- `линия слушалась` rendered as **“the line obeyed.”** Key reduction/relief line.
- `передача орудия состоялась` rendered as **“The transfer of the instrument has taken place.”** Keeps Nick’s procedural irony.
- `родилась пустота, которую теперь надо заполнить` rendered as **“An emptiness has just been born, and now it has to be filled.”** Preserves the white-place danger.
- `белое место` rendered as **white place**, not blank space, to keep the spatial/object pressure.
- `каждая пустота, получив форму, начинала ждать` rendered as **“Every emptiness, once it received a form, began to wait.”** Central continuity from bowl/box/white map-place.
- `Может быть` rendered as **“Perhaps”** and preserved as an active instrument.
- `Может быть начало работать как инструмент` rendered as **“‘Perhaps’ had begun to work as an instrument.”**
- `режет то, чего ещё нет` rendered as **“cuts what is not there yet.”** Preserves the sharpness of speculative possibility.
- Ancient board vision: `доска` rendered as **board**, `угольные точки` as **charcoal dots**, `зерно` as **grain**.
- `порядок согрел` rendered as **“order warmed.”** Simple and central.
- `На доске не мёрзнут ноги` rendered as **“Your feet don’t freeze on it.”** Preserves the old woman’s correction.
- `Крошка не участвует` rendered as **“The crumb is not participating.”** Important and accepted style from Nick’s voice.
- `мы уже начали` rendered as **“we have already begun.”** Keeps David’s frightening reasonableness.
- `не видеть было труднее` rendered as **“not seeing was harder.”** Preserves the closing psychological pressure.
- Final: `Лес не стал понятнее. Он стал меньше.` rendered as **“The forest had not become clearer. It had become smaller.”** Kept exact.

## Places requiring controller attention

- Check title **Chapter Seven. The Map**. It is direct and likely right.
- Check **arranged** for `расположены`. It preserves relation/structure but may be slightly dry; that dryness is intentional.
- Check **“it can be connected”** for `можно соединить`; important hinge.
- Check **“The transfer of the instrument has taken place.”** It is humorous and source-faithful, but may sound slightly formal; Nick’s register supports it.
- Check **“white place”** versus **“blank space.”** Current choice preserves spatial pressure and links to “place,” but “blank space” is more idiomatic.
- Check **“Every emptiness, once it received a form, began to wait.”** Strong central line; likely should remain.
- Check **“the map no longer lay simply on the table / It was reflected in the window over the forest.”** Natural and faithful.
- Check **“It looked convincing. That was exactly the bad part.”** Tone and pressure are preserved.
- Check **“Even when you erase, it remains.”** Nora’s line is direct and should work.
- Check **“The forest has simply been issued a format.”** Source-faithful, but bureaucratic oddness may need audit.
- Check **“The forest enter the pencil”** line grammar in context: **“Sofia watched the forest enter the pencil.”** It is strange but intentional.
- Check **“Not a map. / Not yet. / An attempt.”** for `Не карта. Ещё нет. Попытка.` Strong and likely correct.
- Check **“I am connecting what was there”** against `Я соединяю то, что было`; faithful.
- Check **“Your feet don’t freeze on it”** for `На ней не мёрзнут ноги`; important anti-map correction.
- Check **“The crumb is not participating.”** This is funny and exact; likely should remain.

- Words like **report**, **format**, **instrument**, **scheme**, **map**, **diagram** are internal to character speech / object logic, not external narrative mechanics.

- The chapter’s abstraction remains inside Sofia’s perception and the group’s shared interpretive drift.

## Known risks

- **Volume:** preserved; no intentional compression.
- **Humor:** preserved through Nick, Marek, Lina, Nora, and Eva, but kept under the increasing pressure of map-making.
- **Sampo canon:** preserved. Sampo is not found, explained, or located. The chapter shows how mapping creates a false relief and makes the forest smaller.
- **Ivar rule:** preserved. Ivar remains action-based and minimal: repairing a bag strap, saying `On a map it is not wet`, refusing to correct/destroy the map, adding a log.
- **Objectness/map logic:** preserved. Paper, pencil, crumb, grain, board, line, dot, white place, notebook cover, wet newspapers, bread, bowl, and mug remain concrete.
- **English smoothness:** several lines remain intentionally strange to preserve authorial pressure: `the forest enter the pencil`, `white place`, `emptiness... began to wait`, `Perhaps cuts what is not there yet`.
