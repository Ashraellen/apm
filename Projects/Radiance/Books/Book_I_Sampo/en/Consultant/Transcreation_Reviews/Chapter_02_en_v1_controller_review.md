Mandatory micro-corrections:

1. Replace:

```text
under that soft layer that seemed kind only to someone who had never fallen onto northern ground with his knees
```

with:

```text
under that soft layer that seemed kind only to someone who had never fallen to his knees on northern ground
```

Reason: `with his knees` is a Russian calque and does not read as natural literary English.

2. Replace:

```text
it did not sound like a defence of logic, but like a defence of a human being from too easy a judgment.
```

with:

```text
it did not sound like a defence of physics, but like a defence of a human being from too easy a judgment.
```

Reason: Russian source uses `защита физики`, which is deliberately odd and more concrete than `logic`. `Logic` over-normalizes and slightly flattens the line.

3. Replace:

```text
“At this point,” he said, “they had not yet taken Sampo.”
```

with:

```text
“Here,” he said, “they had not yet taken Sampo.”
```

Reason: `At this point` risks sounding like a lecturer/story analyst and slightly pushes Ivar toward explanation. `Here` keeps the line embodied, immediate, and closer to the fire/vision logic.
