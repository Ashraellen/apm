Mandatory micro-corrections:

1. Replace:

```text
By the window, Lina was folding the dry rag. Folded it once. Then a second time.
```

with:

```text
By the window, Lina was folding the dry rag. Once. Then a second time.
```

Reason: the current fragment `Folded it once` is slightly broken in English. The correction keeps the slow domestic gesture.

2. Replace:

```text
But on the bottom there remained a cloudy stripe — the memory of yesterday’s water, which David had carried to the bushes.
```

with:

```text
But at the bottom there remained a cloudy stripe — the memory of yesterday’s water, which David had carried to the bushes.
```

Reason: natural English preposition.

3. Replace:

```text
With the forest, which did not come close and still was inside the smell of the house.
```

with:

```text
With the forest, which did not come close and yet was inside the smell of the house.
```

Reason: smoother English while preserving the inside/outside relation.

4. Replace:

```text
Outside the window the water went thinly.
```

with:

```text
Outside the window the water ran in a thin line.
```

Reason: current line is too literal. The correction keeps the sound/scale of `тонко шла вода`.

5. Replace:

```text
Rather: whether now one could avoid hurrying to turn this into a word.
```

with:

```text
Rather: whether it was now possible not to hurry to turn this into a word.
```

Reason: more natural English while preserving the unspoken question Ivar answers.

6. Replace:

```text
The sheet on the table moved slightly from the draft. The edge rose and fell.
```

with:

```text
The sheet on the table moved slightly in a draft of air. The edge rose and fell.
```

Reason: `from the draft` is dangerous here because it can sound like manuscript draft/meta-language. The source means a draught of air.

7. Replace:

```text
Now it did not become clear.

It became tight.
```

with:

```text
Now it did not become clear.

It became too tight.
```

Reason: keeps `тесным` but lands more clearly in English. The tightness ladder that follows must remain intact.
