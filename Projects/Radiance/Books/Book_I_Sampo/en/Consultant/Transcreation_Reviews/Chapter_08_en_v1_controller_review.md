Main risk:
No structural problem. The chapter preserves the movement from the dry white place on the map into the wet, full, resistant physical place in the forest. The real hollow does not match the map and does not become an answer, not because it is empty, but because it is already full of its own life: water, moss, decay, insects, mushrooms, leaves, smell, wet earth, and work. The map wants correction; the place simply is. Sampo is named as the thing they are `not` looking for, but it is not found, explained, or located.

Mandatory micro-corrections:

1. Replace:

```text
He was not looking too carefully.
```

with:

```text
He was very carefully not looking.
```

Reason: the current line can mean that David is looking carelessly. The Russian means the opposite: he is deliberately and carefully not looking at the notebook.

2. Replace:

```text
because the moss forgives slowly.
```

with:

```text
because moss forgives slowly.
```

Reason: the source generalizes `мох`; no article is needed. The bare form is stronger and more proverbial.

3. Replace:

```text
I am not saying something must be here.
```

with:

```text
I am not saying there has to be something here.
```

Reason: the replacement is more natural spoken English for David while keeping his defensive logic.

4. Replace:

```text
He waited until the pain itself stopped holding on to the head.
```

with:

```text
He waited until the pain itself stopped holding on by the nailhead.
```

Reason: `the head` is too ambiguous in English and can be read as a human head. The Russian image is explicitly the head of a nail / `шляпка`. The replacement preserves the nail-board image and prevents misreading.

5. Replace:

```text
From the place, which had remained outside and was already again occupied with water under moss?
```

with:

```text
From the place, which had remained outside and was already busy again with water under moss?
```

Reason: `occupied with` is understandable but slightly stiff and may imply occupation by an outside force. `Busy again with` better preserves the place as living in its own work.

6. Replace:

```text
David covered the page with the cover after all.
```

with:

```text
David covered the page with the notebook cover after all.
```

Reason: avoids a small ambiguity/repetition issue around `cover` and makes the physical action clearer.
