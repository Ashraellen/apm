Mandatory micro-corrections:

1. Replace:

```text
David was trying to find a place where his phone would at least imitate signal, and stood by the window with one hand raised, looking like a person receiving a message from a very ancient and dissatisfied civilization.
```

with:

```text
David was trying to find a place where his phone would at least pretend to have a signal, and stood by the window with one hand raised, looking like a person receiving a signal from a very ancient and dissatisfied civilization.
```

Reason: `imitate signal` is not natural English here, and `message` weakens the phone/signal joke. The Russian line works through `связь / сигнал`; keep that field.

2. Replace:

```text
“Not for reserve,” Nora said.
```

with:

```text
“Not as a reserve,” Nora said.
```

Reason: `Not for reserve` is too broken in English. `Not as a reserve` preserves Nora’s odd simplicity while remaining natural enough.

3. Replace:

```text
One could turn it into reserve, into shame, into warm waiting.
```

with:

```text
One could turn it into a reserve, into shame, into warm waiting.
```

Reason: `reserve` needs the article here, especially because Chapter 03 final established `a reserve` in the closing logic.
