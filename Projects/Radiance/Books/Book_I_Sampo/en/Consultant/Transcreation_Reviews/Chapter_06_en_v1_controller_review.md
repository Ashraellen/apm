Mandatory micro-corrections:

1. Replace:

```text
In the morning it had been damp. Now it had become recording.
```

with:

```text
In the morning it had been damp. Now it had become a recording surface.
```

Reason: `had become recording` is too grammatically strained in English. The replacement preserves `стала записывающей` and keeps the path as a surface that receives pressure, without sounding like an error.

2. Replace:

```text
“Does signal love the persistent?”
```

with:

```text
“Does the signal love the persistent?”
```

Reason: the article makes the line natural English while keeping Nick’s dry abstraction.

3. Replace:

```text
“If you walk normally — no. If you look for an adventure — far.”
```

with:

```text
“If you walk normally — no. If you’re looking for adventure — far.”
```

Reason: `look for an adventure` is slightly too soft/idiomatic in the wrong way. The replacement keeps Marek’s dry practical rhythm.

4. Replace:

```text
He said it so quietly that the phrase had to be not so much heard as picked up from the air.
```

with:

```text
He said it so quietly that the phrase had to be picked up from the air rather than heard.
```

Reason: the current version is faithful but syntactically heavy. The replacement preserves the quietness of Ivar’s line with cleaner English rhythm.

5. Replace:

```text
Not as at a student.

As at a person already standing in her own difficulty and best not interfered with.
```

with:

```text
Not the way one looks at a student.

The way one looks at a person already standing inside her own difficulty, and better not interfered with.
```

Reason: `Not as at a student` is too calqued. The replacement preserves Ivar’s non-guru position and the important line that he does not treat Sofia as a pupil.

6. Replace the final line:

```text
But they had already had time to decide that it could.
```

with:

```text
But they had already managed to decide that it could.
```

Reason: the current final line is faithful but slightly cumbersome in English. The replacement keeps the closing pressure and improves final cadence.
